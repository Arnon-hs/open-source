## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

The "who-is-hiring" repository on rebase-network is an open-source initiative that provides a platform for companies and teams in the blockchain and AI/ML industries to share job openings with the Rebase community, free of charge. All job listings will be promoted across Rebase's media channels, connecting job seekers with top industry employers. This platform aims to facilitate career opportunities and industry growth.
