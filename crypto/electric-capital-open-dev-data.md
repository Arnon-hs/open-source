## 📝 Summary

electric‑capital/open‑dev‑data provides a structured taxonomy that catalogues open‑source projects across cryptocurrency, blockchain, and broader decentralized ecosystems. By organizing repositories, protocols, tools, and community resources into clear categories and metadata, it helps developers, researchers, and analysts discover, compare, and integrate open‑source components for building decentralized applications. The project is aimed at improving data accessibility and interoperability within the crypto and emerging AI/ML‑enabled blockchain space.
