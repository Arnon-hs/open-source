Here's a brief summary for the NL Wallet project:

The NL Wallet is an open-source digital identity wallet developed by MinBZK, providing a secure platform for individuals to manage their government-issued digital identities. This wallet ensures a convenient and private way to access various government services and transactions. By utilizing blockchain technology, the NL Wallet enhances the security and integrity of digital identities.
