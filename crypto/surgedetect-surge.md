## 📝 Summary

SurgeDetect/Surge is an open-source project that utilizes machine learning to identify unusual volume spikes on the Solana decentralized exchange (DEX). The project leverages Claude, a natural language processing model, to classify the detected volume spikes as either organic or manipulated, providing insights into potential market manipulation. This tool aims to enhance transparency and fairness in the Solana DEX ecosystem.
