## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the Nexus project:

Nexus is an open-source, cross-chain bridge monitor for the Solana blockchain, designed to track asset flows between Solana and EVM (Ethereum Virtual Machine) chains. Leveraging AI and machine learning technology from Claude, Nexus detects anomalies in these asset flows, providing valuable insights for users and enhancing the overall security and transparency of cross-chain transactions. This project aims to facilitate more secure and efficient cross-chain interactions.
