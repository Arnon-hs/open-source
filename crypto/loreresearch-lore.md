## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the LoreResearch/Lore project:

Lore is an open-source, DeFi protocol research agent built on Solana that utilizes AI and machine learning capabilities to evaluate and score various protocols across key metrics such as security, traction, tokenomics, team, and competitive advantage. This agent leverages Claude, an AI model, to provide objective and data-driven insights to users. By providing a comprehensive evaluation framework, Lore aims to facilitate informed decision-making in the DeFi space.
