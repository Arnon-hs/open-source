Here's a brief summary for the MathParser.org-mXparser project:

MathParser.org-mXparser is an open-source math parsing library available for various programming languages, including Java, C#, C++, Kotlin, and .NET platforms. It offers a rich library of math functions, operators, and constants, along with customizable user-defined arguments and functions. This easy-to-use library supports a wide range of development environments and frameworks, making it a versatile tool for various applications, including AI/ML and frontend development.
