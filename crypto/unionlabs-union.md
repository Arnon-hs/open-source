## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the unionlabs/union project:

Union is an open-source, trust-minimized bridging protocol that enables secure, decentralized transactions in finance. Built with censorship resistance and extreme security in mind, it utilizes zero-knowledge proofs to ensure the integrity and confidentiality of transactions. This protocol aims to provide a robust foundation for decentralized finance applications.
