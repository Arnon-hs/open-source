## 📝 Summary

OnChainMee / OnChaiMee‑Web3‑Portfolio is an open‑source toolkit for Solana‑EVM developers that combines AI agents with on‑chain automation to build and manage a wide range of decentralized finance and Web3 applications. It provides ready‑made smart‑contract libraries, dev‑tools, and infrastructure for DeFi protocols, cross‑chain bridges, NFT marketplaces, token launchpads, GameFi, non‑custodial casinos, staking/yield systems, and institutional lending/RWA solutions. The project aims to accelerate rapid prototyping and deployment of secure, AI‑enhanced blockchain products across multiple domains.
