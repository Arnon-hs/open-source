## 📝 Summary

Covenant is an open‑source payment rail designed for AI agents, enabling autonomous, trust‑less compensation on Solana. Jobs are settled optimistically and auto‑finalized after a challenge window, while any disputes are escalated to a bonded arbitrator for resolution. The protocol works uniformly for any agent, providing a unified, decentralized payment layer for AI‑driven services.
