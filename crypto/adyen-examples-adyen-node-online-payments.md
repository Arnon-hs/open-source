## 📝 Summary

Here is a brief summary for the open-source project:

This open-source project, adyen-node-online-payments, provides an example of integrating Adyen's payment processing capabilities with Node.js and Express-based websites, enabling the acceptance of card payments, wallets, and local payment methods. It allows developers to implement a seamless online payment experience for their users. With this project, developers can easily integrate secure and reliable payment processing into their applications.
