## 📝 Summary

The argotorg/sourcify project provides an open-source service for verifying Ethereum smart contract source code, ensuring the integrity and authenticity of on-chain contracts. This verification service helps maintain trust and transparency in the Ethereum ecosystem by linking on-chain bytecode to its original source code. By verifying source code, developers can ensure that deployed smart contracts match their intended behavior.
