## 📝 Summary

bisq-network/bisq is an open‑source, peer‑to‑peer platform that enables users to trade Bitcoin and other cryptocurrencies without relying on a centralized exchange or custodial services. It operates on a fully decentralized network, using cryptographic protocols and multi‑signature escrow to ensure secure, private, and censorship‑resistant transactions. The project is geared toward crypto traders seeking a trust‑less, self‑custodial trading solution.
