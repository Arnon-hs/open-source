## 📝 Summary

Nervos CKB (Common Knowledge Base) is an open‑source, permissionless layer‑1 blockchain that powers the Nervos Network. It provides a secure, immutable ledger designed for scalable decentralized applications, leveraging a unique cell model and proof‑of‑work consensus to enable flexible asset representation and composable smart contracts.
