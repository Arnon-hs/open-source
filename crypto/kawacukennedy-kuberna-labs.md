## 📝 Summary

kuberna‑labs provides a decentralized execution framework that lets AI agents operate autonomously across multiple blockchains. By combining intent‑based orchestration with zero‑knowledge TLS (zkTLS), the platform enables secure, trust‑less cross‑chain transactions and coordination without relying on a central authority. It targets the crypto, orchestration, and AI/ML ecosystems, offering open‑source tooling for building scalable, privacy‑preserving AI‑driven workflows.
