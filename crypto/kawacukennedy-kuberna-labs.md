## 📝 Summary

Kuberna Labs is an open-source project that facilitates decentralized execution rails for AI agents, empowering autonomous cross-chain transactions through its innovative use of intents and zkTLS (zero-knowledge Transport Layer Security). This cutting-edge solution enables seamless interactions between AI agents and blockchain networks, unlocking new possibilities for AI-driven decentralized applications. By harnessing the power of AI and cryptography, Kuberna Labs aims to revolutionize the way we interact with blockchain technology.
