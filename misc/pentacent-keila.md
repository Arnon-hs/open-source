## 📝 Summary

Keila is an open-source newsletter tool that provides a robust and customizable platform for creating and managing newsletters. With its user-friendly interface, Keila allows users to design visually appealing newsletters, track engagement metrics, and automate workflows. This free and accessible solution empowers individuals and organizations to create professional-looking newsletters without requiring extensive technical expertise.
