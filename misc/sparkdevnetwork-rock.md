## 📝 Summary

Rock is an open-source, all-in-one platform that combines the functions of a Content Management System (CMS), Relationship Management System (RMS), and Church Management System (ChMS). This versatile tool is designed to streamline tasks, manage relationships, and facilitate church operations in an efficient and organized manner. With Rock, users can access a wide range of features to suit their needs.
