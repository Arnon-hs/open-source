## 📝 Summary

Pokémon Showdown is a free, open-source Pokémon battle simulator designed to mimic the gameplay of the popular Pokémon franchise. This versatile tool allows users to engage in turn-based battles, explore various game modes, and even participate in competitive tournaments. With its robust features and customizable settings, Pokémon Showdown has become a go-to platform for fans and trainers alike.
