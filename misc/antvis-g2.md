G2 is an open-source visualization grammar that provides a concise and progressive approach to data visualization. It offers a flexible framework for creating custom visualizations, enabling users to express complex data analysis and representation in a simple and declarative way. With G2, developers can easily create a wide range of charts, graphs, and other visualization elements.
