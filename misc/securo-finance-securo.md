## 📝 Summary

Securo is a self‑hosted, open‑source personal finance manager that puts user privacy at the forefront. It lets individuals track income, expenses, budgets, and financial goals without relying on third‑party services, ensuring all data remains under the user's control. Designed for easy deployment on personal servers, Securo offers a lightweight, customizable alternative to cloud‑based finance apps.
