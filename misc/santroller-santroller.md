## 📝 Summary

The Santroller project is an open-source firmware designed for utilizing various microcontrollers as versatile controllers for a range of applications. This platform enables users to harness the capabilities of different microcontrollers and turn them into adaptable controllers for custom projects, providing flexibility and customization options. With Santroller, users can leverage the power of microcontrollers to create complex control systems and applications.
