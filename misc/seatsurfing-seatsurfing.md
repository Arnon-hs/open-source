## 📝 Summary

The seatsurfing project is an open-source solution designed to facilitate desk sharing, room reservation, and free seating for co-working spaces in enterprises. This platform enables employees to reserve and collaborate on shared workspaces, promoting flexibility and productivity in the office environment. By streamlining workspace management, seatsurfing aims to enhance the overall co-working experience and optimize resource utilization.
