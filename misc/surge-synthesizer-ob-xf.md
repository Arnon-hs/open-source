## 📝 Summary

OB-Xf is a modern, open-source recreation of the Oberheim OB-X synthesizer, built upon the last open-source release of OB-Xd. This project aims to continue and improve upon the original work, bringing the iconic sound of the OB-X to a new generation of musicians and synthesists. By downloading the nightly release, users can access a faithful digital rendition of this legendary analog synthesizer.
