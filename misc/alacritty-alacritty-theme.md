## 📝 Summary

This project, alacritty/alacritty-theme, is an open-source collection of customizable color schemes for Alacritty, a highly customizable terminal emulator. Users can browse and apply various themes to enhance their terminal experience, tailoring it to their preferences and visual style. The repository provides a wide range of pre-designed color schemes to suit different tastes and requirements.
