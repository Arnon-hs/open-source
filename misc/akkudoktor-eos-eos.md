## 📝 Summary

The Akkudoktor-EOS project is an open-source Energy Optimization System designed to optimize energy distribution and usage for various household devices, including batteries and heat pumps. This system leverages predictive models for electricity prices, load forecasting, and dynamic optimization to maximize energy efficiency and minimize costs. Developed by Dr. Andreas Schmitz, the EOS aims to provide a sustainable and cost-effective solution for energy management.
