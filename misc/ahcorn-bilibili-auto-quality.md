## 📝 Summary

AHCorn/Bilibili‑Auto‑Quality is an open‑source tool that automatically unlocks and upgrades the playback settings of Bilibili videos and live streams. It detects the available streams and switches them to the highest possible video resolution, lossless audio, and Dolby Atmos/Dolby Vision when supported, providing a seamless “best‑quality” viewing experience. The project is written in Python/JavaScript (depending on implementation) and can be integrated into browsers or used as a standalone script.
