## 📝 Summary

FlexGet is an open-source project that automates the process of downloading and managing various types of media content, such as TV shows, movies, and podcasts. This powerful tool uses customizable rules to fetch and organize files from various sources, including BitTorrent, Usenet, and HTTP. With FlexGet, users can easily manage their media collections and stay up-to-date with their favorite content.
