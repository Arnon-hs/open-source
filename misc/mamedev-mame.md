## 📝 Summary

MAME (Multiple Arcade Machine Emulator) is an open-source project that aims to preserve the history of video games by emulating a wide range of classic arcade machines and other retro gaming systems. The project provides a comprehensive and accurate emulation of original hardware, allowing users to play and study classic games on modern computers. With its extensive library of supported titles and continuous development, MAME remains a vital resource for gamers, historians, and enthusiasts of classic arcade culture.
