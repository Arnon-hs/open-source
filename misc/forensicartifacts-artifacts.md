## 📝 Summary

The ForensicArtifacts/artifacts project is an open-source digital forensics repository that provides a centralized collection of artifacts from various digital sources, including operating systems, applications, and devices. This comprehensive repository aims to assist investigators and researchers in identifying and analyzing digital evidence, facilitating the process of digital forensic investigations. By consolidating known artifacts, the project promotes knowledge sharing and collaboration in the field of digital forensics.
