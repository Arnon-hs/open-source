## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, Philippe Majerus's ANSI-art collection, is a repository of visually striking ASCII art created using ANSI escape codes. The collection showcases a variety of artistic designs, ranging from simple shapes to complex graphics, all rendered using characters that can be displayed on a terminal or command line. It's a unique digital art form that can be viewed and appreciated on any device with a terminal or command-line interface.
