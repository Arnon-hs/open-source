## 📝 Summary

OctoDash is an open-source, visually appealing dashboard designed specifically for OctoPrint, providing users with a simple yet efficient interface to monitor and control their 3D printing operations. This project offers an alternative to the standard OctoPrint interface, allowing users to customize their experience and streamline their workflow. With its user-friendly design, OctoDash makes it easy to stay on top of your 3D printing projects.
