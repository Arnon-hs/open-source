## 📝 Summary

RetroPie/RetroPie-Setup is a free, open-source project that simplifies the process of setting up a retro gaming station on a Raspberry Pi, Odroid, or PC. This shell script sets up a system with the RetroArch emulator and various cores, allowing users to play classic games from older consoles and systems. With RetroPie, users can easily access a wide range of retro games on their device.
