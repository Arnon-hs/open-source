## 📝 Summary

uBlock Origin is a free, open-source ad blocker designed for Chromium and Firefox browsers. This efficient extension is optimized for speed and minimal system resource usage, making it an ideal choice for users seeking to block unwanted content while preserving their browsing experience. With its lean design and robust features, uBlock Origin provides a seamless way to block ads, scripts, and trackers.
