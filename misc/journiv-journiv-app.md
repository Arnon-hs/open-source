## 📝 Summary

Journiv is an open-source, self-hosted private journaling app that allows users to maintain their personal thoughts and reflections in a secure, decentralized environment. This application provides a safe space for individuals to express themselves without worrying about data breaches or external access. By hosting Journiv locally, users maintain complete control over their journal entries and personal data.
