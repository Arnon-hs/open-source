## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the Neutree/COMTool open-source project:

Neutree/COMTool is a cross-platform serial, network, and terminal debugging assistant that supports plugin development and customization. This tool is designed to work on a variety of operating systems, including Linux, Windows, macOS, and Raspberry Pi. It aims to provide a flexible and universal platform for debugging and communication tasks.
