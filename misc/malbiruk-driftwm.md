## 📝 Summary

DriftWM is an open‑source Wayland compositor designed around a trackpad‑first workflow, offering an infinite‑canvas environment where users can freely pan, zoom, and arrange windows without the constraints of traditional tiling or stacking layouts. Built with simplicity and fluidity in mind, it provides a minimalist yet powerful interface for developers and power users who prefer gesture‑driven navigation on Wayland.
