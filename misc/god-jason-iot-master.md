## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the "iot-master" project:

The IoT Master is an open-source, free IoT platform that integrates various protocols, including Modbus and leading PLC standards. It supports a range of features, including data collection, formula calculation, scheduling, automatic control, alarm notifications, traffic monitoring, web-based configuration, and remote debugging. This platform is suitable for a wide range of IoT and industrial internet application scenarios.
