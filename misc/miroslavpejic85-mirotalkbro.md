## 📝 Summary

Here is a brief summary of the project:

Mirotalkbro is an open-source, self-hosted WebRTC platform that enables real-time video, audio, and screen streaming to an unlimited number of connected viewers. This platform allows users to broadcast live content securely and privately, making it ideal for various applications such as online events, training sessions, and community gatherings. With Mirotalkbro, users have full control over their streaming experience and can customize it to suit their specific needs.
