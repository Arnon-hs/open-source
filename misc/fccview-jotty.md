## 📝 Summary

Jotty is an open-source, lightweight application for managing personal notes and checklists stored in files. It offers a powerful alternative for users seeking a file-based solution for organizing their thoughts and tasks. With Jotty, users can easily create, edit, and track notes and checklists from a single interface.
