## 📝 Summary

The bas-celik project is an open-source program designed to read and process smart-card documents issued by the government of Serbia. It provides a convenient and accessible way to utilize these documents, offering users a simple interface for managing and interpreting the information stored on the cards. This initiative aims to promote transparency and accessibility to official documents in Serbia.
