## 📝 Summary

sprout2000/leafview is a lightweight, cross‑platform image viewer that combines the mapping capabilities of Leaflet.js with the desktop packaging of Electron. It provides a minimalist interface for loading, zooming, and panning high‑resolution images—ideal for developers and hobbyists who need a simple, extensible viewer without unnecessary bloat.
