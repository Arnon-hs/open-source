## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the gin-contrib/logger open-source project:

The gin-contrib/logger project is an open-source middleware/handler that integrates Gin with the zerolog logging library, providing a flexible and efficient way to log URL paths in Gin applications. This middleware allows developers to easily track and monitor their application's URL paths, making it a valuable tool for debugging and troubleshooting. By leveraging the power of zerolog, gin-contrib/logger offers customizable and structured logging for Gin applications.
