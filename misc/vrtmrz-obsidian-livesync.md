## 📝 Summary

**Obsidian LiveSync** is an open‑source plugin that enables real‑time, bidirectional synchronization of an Obsidian vault across multiple devices and platforms. It watches for file changes, resolves conflicts, and streams updates instantly, allowing users to work collaboratively or keep their notes consistently up‑to‑date without manual syncing.
