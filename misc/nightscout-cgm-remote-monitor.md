## 📝 Summary

The nightscout/cgm-remote-monitor project is an open-source initiative that provides a web-based monitoring system, allowing users to remotely track their glucose levels from a continuous glucose monitor (CGM). This system offers a secure and accessible way for individuals with diabetes to monitor their glucose levels and make informed decisions about their care. By leveraging open-source technology, the project promotes collaboration and community-driven development of a critical resource for diabetes management.
