## 📝 Summary

The flake-checker is an open-source project designed to provide health checks for Nix flakes, helping users identify and resolve issues with their flake configurations. This tool aims to ensure that Nix flake environments are properly set up and configured, facilitating smoother package management and development workflows. By leveraging flake-checker, users can catch potential problems early on and maintain a more stable and efficient Nix ecosystem.
