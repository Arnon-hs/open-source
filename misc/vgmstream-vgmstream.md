## 📝 Summary

VGMstream is an open-source library that allows for the playback of various streamed audio formats commonly used in video games. It provides a flexible and efficient way to decode and render a wide range of audio formats, making it a valuable tool for game preservation and development. The library is designed to be extensible and easily customizable, supporting a diverse collection of video game audio formats.
