## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, dext0r/yandex_smart_home, enables integration of Home Assistant with Yandex Smart Home devices, such as Alice (Yandex Smart Home) or Marusya. It allows users to control their devices from within Home Assistant, providing a seamless smart home experience. This integration supports devices from various manufacturers, making it a valuable addition to the Home Assistant ecosystem.
