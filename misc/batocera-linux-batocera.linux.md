## 📝 Summary

Batocera.linux is an open‑source, community‑driven operating system that transforms a PC, single‑board computer, or embedded device into a plug‑and‑play retro gaming console. Built on a lightweight Linux base, it bundles a wide range of emulators, game libraries, and front‑end tools, enabling users to easily browse and play classic arcade, console, and computer titles from a single, customizable interface.
