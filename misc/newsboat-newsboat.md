## 📝 Summary

Newsboat is a free, open-source RSS/Atom feed reader designed for use in text terminals. It allows users to easily subscribe to and manage their favorite news feeds, displaying articles in a clean and customizable interface. Newsboat is ideal for those seeking a lightweight and efficient feed reader for their command-line environment.
