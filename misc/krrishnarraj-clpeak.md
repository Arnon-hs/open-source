## 📝 Summary

The clpeak project is an open-source, synthetic micro-benchmark designed to measure the peak achievable performance of GPU compute devices. It serves as a tool for developers and researchers to gauge the raw computational capabilities of their GPU hardware, providing insights into the theoretical maximum performance. By utilizing clpeak, users can optimize their applications and workloads for optimal GPU utilization.
