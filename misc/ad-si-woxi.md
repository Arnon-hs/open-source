## 📝 Summary

Woxi is an open‑source effort to recreate the Wolfram Language/Mathematica core in Rust, offering a “Wolfram oxidized” interpreter and runtime. By re‑implementing the language’s symbolic computation engine, parsing, and evaluation model in a safe, high‑performance systems language, the project aims to provide a compatible, extensible alternative to the proprietary Mathematica platform. It targets developers and researchers who need a free, cross‑platform Wolfram Language environment with the benefits of Rust’s memory safety and concurrency features.
