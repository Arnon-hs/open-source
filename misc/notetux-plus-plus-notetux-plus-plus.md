## 📝 Summary

Notetux++ is an open‑source, native Linux text editor that brings the look and feel of Notepad++ to the GTK3 desktop environment. Written in modern C11, it offers the familiar multi‑tab, syntax‑highlighting and plugin capabilities of its Windows counterpart while integrating tightly with Linux themes and system libraries. The project is licensed under an open‑source license and is maintained as a community‑driven alternative for developers who prefer a lightweight, GTK‑based editor.
