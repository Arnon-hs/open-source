## 📝 Summary

The nasin-nanpa project is an open-source initiative that utilizes the sitelen tan anpa (a script of the pidgin language Toki Pona) to convey complex information in a simplified and intuitive manner. This project seeks to promote clarity and understanding through a unique visual representation of concepts. By harnessing the expressive power of sitelen tan anpa, nasin-nanpa aims to facilitate knowledge sharing and collaboration among users.
