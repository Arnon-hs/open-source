## 📝 Summary

**studyzy/imewlconverter** is an open‑source, free utility for converting input‑method dictionaries, specifically designed for the “深蓝词库” (Deep Blue) format. It provides a simple command‑line interface to transform word‑list files between various IME (Input Method Editor) specifications, making it easier to share and reuse custom vocabularies across different Chinese input methods. The project is categorized under Miscellaneous and is released under an open‑source license.
