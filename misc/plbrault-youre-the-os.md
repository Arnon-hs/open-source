## 📝 Summary

In "you're the OS," an open-source game, players take on the role of a computer's operating system, tasked with efficiently managing system resources, including processes, memory, and I/O events. The game simulates real-world operating system challenges, requiring players to make decisions that balance performance and resource allocation. By navigating these complexities, players gain insight into the inner workings of operating systems.
