## 📝 Summary

Po1nt9/Claude-Desktop-Chinese is an open‑source localization patch that fully translates the Claude Desktop AI client into Chinese, covering chat interfaces, settings, error messages, and the Cowork workspace. It also adds support for custom fonts, allowing users to tailor the visual appearance to their preferences. The project is categorized under Miscellaneous.
