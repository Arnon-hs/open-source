## 📝 Summary

Show HN presents an open‑source tool that extracts years of “Ask HN” and “Show HN” threads from Hacker News and transforms them into a local knowledge graph. By parsing the posts and their metadata, it builds a searchable graph database that lets users explore relationships, trends, and recurring topics across the community’s history. The project is a lightweight, language‑agnostic solution for anyone who wants to analyze HN discussions offline or integrate the data into other applications.
