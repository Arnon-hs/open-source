## 📝 Summary

The instructure/canvas-lms project is an open-source Learning Management System (LMS) developed by Instructure, Inc. It provides a centralized platform for instructors to create, manage, and deliver online courses, assignments, and assessments to students. Canvas LMS offers a user-friendly interface and a wide range of features to facilitate effective teaching and learning.
