## 📝 Summary

Carapace is an open-source, multi-shell completion library designed to provide a unified and extensible way to manage shell completions. It aims to simplify the development and integration of shell completions across various shells and platforms. By providing a shared library for completion functionality, Carapace facilitates the creation of consistent and user-friendly command-line interfaces.
