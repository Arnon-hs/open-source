## 📝 Summary

Kotlin/KEEP is an open-source project that outlines the evolution and enhancement process of the Kotlin programming language. It provides a structured framework for proposing, discussing, and implementing changes to Kotlin, ensuring transparency and community involvement in its development. Through KEEP, the Kotlin community can contribute to shaping the language's future and influence its growth.
