## 📝 Summary

A lightweight open‑source utility that scans a codebase and reports the call‑graph relationships between functions, showing both the callers and the actual implementers. It’s designed for quick insight into who invokes a given function and where its definition resides, making navigation and code reviews easier. Ideal for developers who need a fast, no‑frills way to understand function dependencies in any project.
