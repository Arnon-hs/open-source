Arcthesia/ArcCreate is an open‑source, Unity‑based tool that lets players both edit and play custom rhythm game charts. It provides a community‑driven platform for creating, sharing, and experiencing rhythm‑game content, with a focus on ease of use and extensibility. The project is licensed for free modification and distribution, encouraging collaborative development and new gameplay experiences.
