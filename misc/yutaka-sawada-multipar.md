## 📝 Summary

MultiPar is an open-source Parchive tool designed to facilitate multi-volume archives. It aims to provide a robust solution for creating and managing PAR2 files, which are commonly used for data recovery and integrity verification in multi-volume archives. By leveraging this tool, users can ensure the reliability and integrity of their data by creating and verifying PAR2 files.
