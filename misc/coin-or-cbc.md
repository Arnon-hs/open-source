## 📝 Summary

The coin-or/Cbc project is an open-source, Branch-and-Cut solver that uses a combination of branch-and-bound and cutting plane techniques to solve complex optimization problems. This solver is part of the COIN-OR (Computational Infrastructure for Operations Research) initiative and provides a powerful tool for tackling various types of mixed-integer programming problems. By leveraging the strengths of both Branch-and-Bound and cutting planes, Cbc offers an efficient and effective solution for a wide range of optimization challenges.
