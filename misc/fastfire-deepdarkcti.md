## 📝 Summary

The deepdarkCTI project is an open-source collection of Cyber Threat Intelligence (CTI) sources gathered from the deep and dark web. This repository aims to provide researchers, security professionals, and analysts with a centralized platform to access and analyze threat intelligence from these often-inaccessible online environments. By aggregating this information, deepdarkCTI facilitates a better understanding of cyber threats and their actors.
