## 📝 Summary

am-will/limux is a lightweight, GPU‑accelerated terminal multiplexer designed for Linux environments. It leverages modern graphics hardware to render multiple terminal sessions with minimal latency and reduced CPU load, offering smooth scrolling, high‑resolution fonts, and efficient pane management. Ideal for developers and power users who need fast, visually rich multiplexing without sacrificing system resources.
