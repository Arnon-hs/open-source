## 📝 Summary

The **Launcher** project provides a lightweight, customizable firmware bootloader for ESP32‑based devices such as M5Stack, Lilygo, Marauder and CYD boards. It simplifies flashing and managing multiple firmware images, offering a user‑friendly interface and support for OTA updates, making development and deployment on these ESP32 platforms faster and more reliable.
