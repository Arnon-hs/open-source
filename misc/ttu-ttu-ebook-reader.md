## 📝 Summary

The ttu-ttu/ebook-reader is an open-source online e-book reader designed to provide a seamless reading experience. It supports Yomichan, a popular Japanese dictionary tool, allowing users to look up words and phrases in real-time as they read. This feature-rich e-reader is a valuable resource for anyone looking to enhance their reading experience, especially for those interested in Japanese literature and language.
