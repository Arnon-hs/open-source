## 📝 Summary

OpenTripPlanner is a free and open-source, multi-modal trip planning system that allows users to plan complex trips across various transportation modes, including walking, biking, driving, and public transit. This platform provides an intuitive interface for users to input their origin and destination, and offers optimized routes with estimated travel times and distances. With OpenTripPlanner, developers can integrate real-time transit data and customize trip planning services to suit their needs.
