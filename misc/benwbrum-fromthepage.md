## 📝 Summary

FromThePage is an open-source, wiki-like application designed for collaborative crowdsourcing of handwritten document transcription. This platform enables teams of volunteers to work together to transcribe and preserve historical documents, promoting accessibility and historical research. By leveraging community contributions, FromThePage facilitates the digitization and analysis of valuable handwritten materials.
