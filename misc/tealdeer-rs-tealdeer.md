## 📝 Summary

Tealdeer is a fast and efficient Rust implementation of the tldr command-line tool. It retrieves and displays concise summaries of terminal commands, helping users quickly understand complex commands and their usage. By leveraging Rust's performance capabilities, Tealdeer aims to provide a speedy and user-friendly experience for navigating terminal commands.
