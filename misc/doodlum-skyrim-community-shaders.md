## 📝 Summary

The Skyrim Community Shaders project is an open-source initiative that brings advanced graphics modifications to the popular game The Elder Scrolls V: Skyrim, including its original edition (AE), Special Edition (SE), and VR versions. This community-driven effort provides users with a wide range of customizable graphics options, enhancing the overall gaming experience. By offering advanced shader capabilities, the project empowers users to personalize their game's visuals.
