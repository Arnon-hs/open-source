## 📝 Summary

'A List of Post-Mortems' is an open-source project that collects and shares post-mortem reports from various companies and organizations. These reports provide detailed analyses of project failures, offering valuable insights into the factors that contributed to their demise and lessons learned from the experience. The goal of the project is to help others learn from these failures and avoid similar mistakes in their own endeavors.
