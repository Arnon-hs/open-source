## 📝 Summary

Stremio is a free and open-source media player that allows users to stream their favorite content from various sources, including Netflix, Amazon Prime, and more. With its user-friendly interface and extensive library of plugins, Stremio gives users the freedom to access a wide range of streaming services and media content in one convenient platform. Its open-source nature also enables community-driven development and customization.
