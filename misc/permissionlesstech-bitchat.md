## 📝 Summary

Bitchat is an open-source project that enables Bluetooth mesh-based communication, offering a chat experience reminiscent of classic IRC (Internet Relay Chat) platforms. This project allows users to connect over Bluetooth and engage in real-time conversations, leveraging mesh networking for efficient and reliable communication. By providing a unique blend of Bluetooth connectivity and IRC-inspired functionality, bitchat opens up new possibilities for wireless communication and collaboration.
