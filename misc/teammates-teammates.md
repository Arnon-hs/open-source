## 📝 Summary

TEAMMATES is an open-source, web-based platform designed to facilitate peer and self-assessment within educational settings. The tool allows students and instructors to provide and receive constructive feedback, promoting a collaborative learning environment. This free resource aims to support student growth, reflection, and development through the exchange of feedback and evaluations.
