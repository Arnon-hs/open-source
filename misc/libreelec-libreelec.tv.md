## 📝 Summary

LibreELEC, formerly known as LibreELEC.tv, is a lightweight, open-source operating system designed specifically to run Kodi media centers. This minimalistic OS streamlines the installation process and optimizes performance for Kodi, making it an ideal choice for users seeking a hassle-free entertainment experience. With its focus on simplicity and Kodi compatibility, LibreELEC offers a seamless way to enjoy multimedia content.
