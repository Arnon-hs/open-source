## 📝 Summary

iKineticate/BlueGauge is a lightweight system‑tray utility that lets users quickly check the battery levels of nearby Bluetooth devices such as headphones, keyboards, and mice. It runs silently in the background, periodically queries paired devices, and displays their charge status with simple icons or tooltip text, making battery monitoring effortless. The tool is open‑source, cross‑platform, and designed for easy installation and minimal resource usage.
