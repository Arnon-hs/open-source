## 📝 Summary

wluma is an open-source project that automatically adjusts the brightness of a screen based on its contents and ambient light sensor (ALS) readings. This feature aims to provide a more comfortable viewing experience by dynamically adapting to changing lighting conditions. By optimizing screen brightness in real-time, wluma helps conserve battery life and reduce eye strain.
