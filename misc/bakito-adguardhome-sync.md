## 📝 Summary

The bakito/adguardhome-sync project is an open-source tool designed to synchronize configurations between multiple instances of AdGuard Home. This allows users to maintain identical settings across replicas, simplifying the management of their network's ad-blocking and security features. By automating this process, users can ensure consistent protection across their network.
