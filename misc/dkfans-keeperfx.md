## 📝 Summary

dkfans/keeperfx is an open‑source remake and fan expansion of the classic real‑time strategy game *Dungeon Keeper*. It recreates the original’s core gameplay while adding new features, improvements, and community‑driven content, all under a permissive license. The project aims to preserve the nostalgic experience and enable further development by enthusiasts.
