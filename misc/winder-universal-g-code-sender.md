## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the Universal-G-Code-Sender project:

Universal-G-Code-Sender is an open-source, cross-platform application designed to send G-Code to various firmware platforms, including GRBL, Smoothieware, TinyG, and G2core. This tool enables users to easily communicate with and control their CNC machines or 3D printers using a user-friendly interface. By supporting multiple firmware platforms, it offers a versatile solution for a wide range of CNC and 3D printing applications.
