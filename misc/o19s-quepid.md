## 📝 Summary

Quepid is an open-source project designed to enhance the quality of search results across various search engines, including OpenSearch, Elasticsearch, Solr, Vectara, Algolia, and custom search solutions. It aims to provide a comprehensive and user-friendly interface for evaluating and refining search queries, ultimately resulting in more accurate and relevant search results. By leveraging Quepid, users can optimize their search experience and improve overall search engine performance.
