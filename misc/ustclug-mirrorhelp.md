## 📝 Summary

The ustclug/mirrorhelp project provides official documentation for the USTC Mirrors, a collection of mirror sites for the University of Science and Technology of China (USTC) community. This resource aims to facilitate access to USTC's online services and content by offering a centralized guide to available mirrors. The project contributes to the convenience and accessibility of USTC's online ecosystem.
