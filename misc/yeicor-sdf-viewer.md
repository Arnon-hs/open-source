## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, sdf-viewer, is a fast and cross-platform tool for visualizing Signed Distance Functions (SDFs). Developed by yeicor, it provides an easy way to integrate SDF visualization into your existing SDF library, allowing for efficient and versatile exploration of SDF-based 3D models and environments.
