## 📝 Summary

Media Downloader is an open-source application that provides a user-friendly interface to download multimedia content from various online platforms using a range of command-line tools, including yt-dlp and youtube-dl. This Qt/C++ front end simplifies the process of downloading videos, music, and other media types from popular websites. With its versatility and ease of use, Media Downloader is a valuable tool for anyone looking to manage their digital media collection.
