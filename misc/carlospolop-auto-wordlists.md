## 📝 Summary

Auto_Wordlists is an open‑source collection of curated wordlists designed for use in security testing, password cracking, and data‑matching tasks. Maintained by carlospolop, the repository organizes the lists by purpose and size, providing ready‑to‑use text files that can be easily integrated into tools like Hashcat, John the Ripper, and other enumeration utilities. It serves as a convenient, community‑maintained resource for anyone needing reliable wordlists without having to generate or compile them manually.
