## 📝 Summary

The Confidential Consortium Framework (CCF) is an open-source project developed by Microsoft, designed to provide a secure and decentralized framework for permissioned blockchain networks. CCF enables the creation of confidential and trustworthy networks for businesses and organizations, allowing for private transactions and secure data sharing without the need for a trusted third-party mediator. By leveraging CCF, organizations can build scalable and secure blockchain solutions tailored to their specific needs.
