## 📝 Summary

The better-lyrics project is an open-source initiative that aims to elevate the YouTube Music experience by providing beautifully time-synced lyrics, multilingual support, and real-time translations. This enhancement allows users to enjoy their favorite songs with more depth and understanding, transcending language barriers. By incorporating these features, better-lyrics seeks to create a more engaging and immersive music-listening experience.
