## 📝 Summary

VsTV is an open‑source application that lets users stream live TV channels on smart TVs, set‑top boxes, tablets, or smartphones. It supports a wide range of domestic and popular brands—including TCL, Hisense, Coolpad, Huawei Smart Screen, Honor, Xiaomi, and more—providing a unified viewing experience across devices.
