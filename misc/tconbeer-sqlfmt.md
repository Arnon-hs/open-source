Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the open-source project tconbeer/sqlfmt:

This tool enables users to effortlessly standardize their SQL files to conform to a chosen formatting convention. By streamlining the formatting process, sqlfmt enhances collaboration and readability among team members working on dbt projects.
