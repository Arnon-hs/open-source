## 📝 Summary

The Cursorless project aims to eliminate the need for a cursor in text editing, providing a distraction-free writing experience. This open-source initiative is designed to enhance productivity by allowing users to focus on their content without visual interruptions. By removing the cursor, users can concentrate on their work with increased efficiency and reduced visual noise.
