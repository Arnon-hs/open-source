## 📝 Summary

sevagh/pq is a lightweight command‑line tool that parses Protocol Buffer (Protobuf) messages, optionally consuming them from Kafka topics. It converts the decoded data into human‑readable JSON, making it easy to inspect, debug, or pipe Protobuf payloads into downstream processes. The utility is open‑source, cross‑platform, and suited for quick Protobuf handling without writing custom code.
