## 📝 Summary

This open-source project is designed to manage and optimize a TV show's episode library by removing low-quality episodes, retaining the best versions, and handling associated sidecars. The tool aims to provide a streamlined experience for users seeking a high-quality collection of their favorite shows. It achieves this through automated deduplication based on quality assessments.
