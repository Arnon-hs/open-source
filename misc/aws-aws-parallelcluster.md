## 📝 Summary

AWS ParallelCluster is an open-source, AWS-supported tool for deploying and managing high-performance computing (HPC) clusters in the cloud. This cluster management tool enables users to easily set up, configure, and maintain scalable and secure HPC environments on AWS. By leveraging ParallelCluster, users can streamline their HPC workflows and take advantage of the computational power of the cloud.
