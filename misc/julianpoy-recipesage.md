## 📝 Summary

RecipeSage is an open-source, Progressive Web App (PWA) designed to efficiently manage your culinary needs. This collaborative project allows users to store and organize their personal recipes, plan meals, and create customizable shopping lists, streamlining kitchen tasks and grocery shopping. By utilizing RecipeSage, users can save time and enjoy a more organized culinary experience.
