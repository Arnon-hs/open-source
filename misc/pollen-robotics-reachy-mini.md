## 📝 Summary

The Reachy Mini SDK is an open-source project providing software tools for interacting with the Reachy Mini, a compact robotic arm designed for research, development, and education. This SDK enables developers to leverage the Reachy Mini's capabilities, such as its manipulator, sensors, and communication interfaces, to create innovative applications and experiments. By utilizing this SDK, users can unlock the full potential of the Reachy Mini and push the boundaries of robotics and artificial intelligence.
