## 📝 Summary

The gamdl project is a free, open-source command-line application designed to download Apple Music content, including songs, music videos, and post videos. This tool allows users to access and save Apple Music content without requiring a subscription to the platform. By providing a simple command-line interface, gamdl aims to offer users an alternative way to manage their music collections.
