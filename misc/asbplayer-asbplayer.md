## 📝 Summary

asbplayer is an open‑source, browser‑based media player and Chrome extension designed for subtitle‑driven language learning. It lets users load video or audio files with subtitles, automatically splits the subtitles into individual sentences, and provides tools for easy playback, looping, and export of selected clips for sentence‑mining practice. The project is lightweight, works across major browsers, and is customizable via a simple configuration file.
