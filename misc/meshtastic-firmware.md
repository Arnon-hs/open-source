## 📝 Summary

Meshtastic's open-source firmware enables users to create a decentralized, off-grid mesh network for communication. This system allows devices to connect and relay messages, providing a reliable and secure means of communication in areas with limited or no cellular coverage. By utilizing off-grid mesh technology, users can maintain connectivity in remote or disaster-stricken areas.
