## 📝 Summary

Bevy Seedling is an open-source project that integrates the Firewheel audio engine into the Bevy game engine. This integration is designed to simplify the process of incorporating high-quality audio into Bevy-based projects. By providing a seamless way to access Firewheel's features, Bevy Seedling aims to enhance the audio capabilities of Bevy games and applications.
