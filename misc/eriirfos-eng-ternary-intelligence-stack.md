## 📝 Summary

**Summary:**  
This open‑source project investigates a phenomenon observed in small Mixture‑of‑Experts (MoE) neural networks where the model’s expert modules become overly similar—a problem dubbed “expert homogeneity collapse.” By reproducing and analyzing reports from Hacker News and related GitHub mentions, the project provides diagnostics, visualizations, and potential mitigation strategies to help developers detect and address this collapse in compact MoE architectures.
