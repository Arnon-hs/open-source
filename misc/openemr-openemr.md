## 📝 Summary

OpenEMR is a free, open-source electronic health records (EHR) and medical practice management solution. It provides a comprehensive platform for managing patient data, billing, and other clinical operations, making it an accessible and cost-effective option for healthcare providers. With its modular design and customization capabilities, OpenEMR can be tailored to meet the specific needs of various medical practices.
