## 📝 Summary

This open-source project is a recreation of Martin Galway's music assembly player, which was originally used in Commodore 64 games. The player is designed to accurately render the iconic music compositions created by Galway for classic Commodore 64 games. By providing this assembly player, the project aims to preserve and share the nostalgic sounds of the Commodore 64 era.
