## 📝 Summary

Space Station 14 is a multiplayer game that recreates the chaos and paranoia of the cult-classic Space Station 13 in an open-source format. Players take on various roles within the space station, including crew members, security officers, and aliens, navigating a web of suspicion and intrigue. With a focus on emergent gameplay and community-driven development, Space Station 14 offers a unique and immersive experience for players.
