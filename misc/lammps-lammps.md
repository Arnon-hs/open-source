## 📝 Summary

The LAMMPS (Large-scale Atomic/Molecular Massively Parallel Simulator) project is an open-source molecular dynamics software package. It is designed to simulate the behavior of complex systems, such as materials, molecules, and proteins, using a variety of force fields and simulation algorithms. LAMMPS is widely used in various fields, including materials science, chemistry, and biophysics, for research and educational purposes.
