> PacketFence is a fully supported, trusted, Free and Open Source network access control (NAC) solution. Boasting an impressive feature set including a captive-portal for registration and remediation, centralized wired and wireless management, powerful BYOD management options, 802.1X support, layer-2 isolation of problematic devices; PacketFence can be used to effectively secure networks small to very large heterogeneous networks.

## 📝 Summary

PacketFence is a comprehensive, free, and open-source network access control (NAC) solution. This robust platform offers a range of features, including a captive portal for user registration and remediation, centralized management of wired and wireless networks, and advanced BYOD management options. With its powerful tools, PacketFence effectively secures networks of all sizes, from small to large and heterogeneous networks.
