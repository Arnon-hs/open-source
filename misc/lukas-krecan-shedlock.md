## 📝 Summary

ShedLock is an open-source distributed lock project designed to prevent concurrent execution of scheduled tasks in distributed environments. It ensures that only one instance of a task runs at a time, even across multiple machines or processes. This helps maintain data consistency and prevents potential conflicts or errors.
