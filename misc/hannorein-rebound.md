## 📝 Summary

The rebound project is an open-source, multi-purpose N-body code designed to simulate complex systems of celestial bodies interacting through gravity and other forces. This versatile code can be used to model a wide range of astrophysical phenomena, from planetary orbits to galaxy evolution. By providing a flexible and customizable simulation environment, rebound enables researchers to explore the dynamics of complex systems with unprecedented detail and accuracy.
