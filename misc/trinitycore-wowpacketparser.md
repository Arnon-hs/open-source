## 📝 Summary

TrinityCore/WowPacketParser is an open-source project that provides a World of Warcraft packet parser, allowing developers to decode and analyze network traffic from the popular MMORPG. This parser offers a valuable tool for modifying or extending the game's functionality, as well as for educational purposes. By providing a detailed breakdown of WoW's packet structure, the project facilitates further development and exploration of the game's inner workings.
