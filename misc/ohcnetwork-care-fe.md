## 📝 Summary

Care is an open-source digital public good designed to facilitate remote intensive care unit (TeleICU) services and decentralized administration of healthcare capacity across different states. This platform aims to bridge geographical gaps in healthcare delivery and enable efficient management of resources. By leveraging technology, Care seeks to improve patient care and outcomes in a scalable and accessible manner.
