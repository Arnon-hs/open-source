## 📝 Summary

v ega/vl-convert is an open‑source toolkit that provides command‑line and Python utilities for transforming Vega‑Lite specifications into compiled Vega JSON, SVG, PNG, or PDF outputs. It enables developers to integrate Vega‑Lite rendering into scripts, CI pipelines, or web services without needing a full browser environment, simplifying the generation of static visualizations from declarative specs.
