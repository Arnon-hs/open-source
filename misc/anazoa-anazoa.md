## 📝 Summary

Anazoa WebRTC Tunnel is an open‑source utility that leverages WebRTC data channels to create bidirectional network tunnels, enabling the routing of arbitrary TCP/UDP traffic through peer‑to‑peer connections without needing traditional VPN infrastructure. It is designed for quick setup and cross‑platform use, making it useful for bypassing firewalls, NAT traversal, or establishing low‑latency links between devices.
