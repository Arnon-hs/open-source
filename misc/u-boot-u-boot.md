## 📝 Summary

"U-Boot" is an open-source bootloader that allows users to boot operating systems on embedded systems, such as microcontrollers and single-board computers. It supports a wide range of hardware platforms and provides a flexible and customizable boot process. With a community-driven development model, U-Boot continues to evolve and expand its capabilities.
