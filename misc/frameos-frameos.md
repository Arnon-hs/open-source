## 📝 Summary

FrameOS is an open‑source operating system designed specifically for single‑function smart frames, providing a lightweight, modular platform that handles display rendering, network connectivity, and peripheral control. It enables developers to quickly build and customize embedded frame devices for applications such as digital signage, IoT dashboards, and interactive art installations.
