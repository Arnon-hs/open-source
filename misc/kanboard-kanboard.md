## 📝 Summary

Kanboard is a free, open-source Kanban project management software that enables teams to visualize and manage their workflows, tasks, and projects in a flexible and customizable way. It offers a range of features, including task assignment, due dates, and priorities, as well as integration options with third-party tools and services. With Kanboard, teams can streamline their workflow, increase productivity, and collaborate more effectively.
