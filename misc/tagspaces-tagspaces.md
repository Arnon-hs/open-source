## 📝 Summary

TagSpaces is an open-source, offline document management system that utilizes tagging support to organize digital content. This platform enables users to easily access, manage, and share files across various devices without relying on internet connectivity. With its flexible tagging system, users can efficiently categorize and retrieve documents within the application.
