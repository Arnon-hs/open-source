## 📝 Summary

**RKNHardening** is an open‑source tool that scans user devices for VPN configuration files and other network‑proxy artifacts, aiming to identify and mitigate the presence of VPNs that could be used to bypass internet restrictions. By detecting these configurations, the project helps enforce compliance with regional filtering regulations and reduces the risk of users being flagged for evading censorship.
