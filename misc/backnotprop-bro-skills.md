## 📝 Summary

Bro Skills is an open-source project aimed at addressing the knowledge gaps that customer support agents often face in their daily work. This initiative seeks to bridge the gap by providing essential skills training, thereby enhancing the overall customer experience and improving agent productivity. The project utilizes AI and ML to offer tailored learning paths and support materials to help agents adapt to complex situations.
