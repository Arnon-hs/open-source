## 📝 Summary

Manifold is an open-source platform that enables users to create and trade on markets for various questions, essentially allowing them to wager on the likelihood of different events or outcomes. It provides a space for users to express their opinions and predictions on a wide range of topics, from entertainment to politics and beyond. This platform facilitates the aggregation of collective knowledge and insight through predictive markets.
