## 📝 Summary

Play! is an open‑source emulator that enables PlayStation 2 games to run on non‑Sony hardware. It reproduces the PS2’s CPU, GPU, and peripheral behavior, allowing users to load ISO images or disc dumps and experience classic titles on modern PCs. The project is maintained under the “Misc” category and welcomes contributions from the emulation community.
