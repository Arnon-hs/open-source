## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the daya0576/beaverhabits project:

Beaverhabits is a self-hosted habit tracking application that allows users to monitor and maintain their daily routines without the traditional concept of "goals." This open-source project provides a flexible and customizable platform for individuals to track their habits and develop consistent behaviors. By hosting the app on their own servers, users maintain control over their data and experience.
