## 📝 Summary

The Alaveteli project is an open-source platform designed to facilitate the submission and management of Freedom of Information (FOI) requests within a jurisdiction. This system enables citizens to easily file requests and track their progress, promoting transparency and accountability in government decision-making. By providing a standardized framework, Alaveteli helps jurisdictions streamline their FOI processes and increase public participation.
