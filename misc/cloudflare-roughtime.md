## 📝 Summary

Roughtime is an open-source, secure clock-synchronization protocol developed by Cloudflare. It aims to provide a simple and efficient method for synchronizing system clocks, prioritizing ease of implementation over strict accuracy. By focusing on rough estimates of time rather than precise synchronization, Roughtime offers a scalable and resilient solution for modern applications.
