## 📝 Summary

The schwung project is an open-source framework designed to expand the capabilities of the Ableton M4, providing a collection of additional synthesizers, effects, and tools for musicians and producers. This community-driven initiative aims to enhance the M4's creative potential, offering users a wide range of new possibilities for sound design and music production. By fostering collaboration and innovation, schwung seeks to empower artists and developers alike.
