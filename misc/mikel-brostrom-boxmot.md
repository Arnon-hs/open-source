## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the BoxMOT project:

BoxMOT is an open-source, pluggable framework for multi-object tracking that offers cutting-edge modules with support for both axis-aligned and oriented bounding boxes. This project enables users to seamlessly integrate various state-of-the-art tracking algorithms, enhancing flexibility and adaptability in object tracking applications. BoxMOT is designed to tackle complex scenarios in computer vision, such as detecting and tracking multiple objects across different environments.
