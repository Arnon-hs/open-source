## 📝 Summary

The open-source project "endoflife.date" provides a comprehensive online platform that lists the end-of-life (EoL) dates of various software, hardware, and other digital products. This resource aims to assist users in making informed decisions about updating or replacing their products, ensuring they remain secure and compatible with current systems. By aggregating EoL dates from multiple sources, endoflife.date strives to be a valuable tool for IT professionals, developers, and individuals alike.
