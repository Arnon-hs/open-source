## 📝 Summary

nextcloud/notes is an open‑source, distraction‑free note‑taking and writing app designed to integrate seamlessly with Nextcloud. It lets users create, edit, and organize plain‑text notes directly in the cloud, with automatic synchronization across devices and support for markdown formatting. The lightweight, web‑based interface focuses on simplicity while providing essential features such as tagging, search, and version history for personal or collaborative use.
