## 📝 Summary

OpenModelica is an open-source software that enables the creation and simulation of complex models in various fields, built on the Modelica modeling language. This free and collaborative environment is designed for both industrial and academic applications, allowing users to model, simulate, and analyze dynamic systems efficiently. It supports a wide range of engineering disciplines, including mechanical, electrical, and thermal systems.
