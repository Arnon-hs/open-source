## 📝 Summary

Timeless Jewels is an open-source project that provides a jewel calculator and skill tree for Path of Exile, a popular action RPG. This tool allows players to plan and optimize their builds by visualizing the effects of various jewels on their characters, taking into account various factors such as stats and skill interactions. With Timeless Jewels, players can make informed decisions to create powerful and efficient characters.
