## 📝 Summary

ZNC is an open-source, highly customizable IRC bouncer that enables users to connect to multiple IRC networks simultaneously while maintaining a single, persistent connection. This allows users to stay connected to their favorite IRC channels even when their computer is not online. With its modular design and extensive configuration options, ZNC provides a flexible solution for managing IRC connections.
