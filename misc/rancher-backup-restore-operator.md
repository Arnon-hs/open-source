## 📝 Summary

The rancher/backup-restore-operator is an open-source project that enables automated backup and restore functionality for Kubernetes environments. This operator simplifies data management by creating, managing, and restoring backups of Kubernetes resources, such as pods, services, and persistent volumes. By leveraging this tool, users can ensure business continuity and minimize downtime in case of data loss or system failure.
