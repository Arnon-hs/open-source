## 📝 Summary

PiSCSI is an open-source project that enables a Raspberry Pi to emulate various SCSI devices, including hard disks and CD-ROMs, for vintage SCSI-based computers and devices. This project, a fork of RaSCSI, allows users to breathe new life into legacy systems by providing a cost-effective and accessible solution for emulation. By leveraging a Raspberry Pi, users can create a versatile and portable SCSI emulator for a wide range of applications.
