## 📝 Summary

open-spaced-repetition/fsrs4anki-helper is an open‑source Anki add‑on that extends the FSRS (Free Spaced Repetition Scheduler) functionality with a suite of advanced scheduling tools. It lets users postpone or advance cards, balance daily loads, define “easy days,” disperse sibling cards, and flatten review queues for smoother study sessions. The add‑on is categorized under Miscellaneous utilities.
