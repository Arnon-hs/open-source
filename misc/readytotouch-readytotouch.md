## 📝 Summary

ReadyToTouch is an open-source platform designed to streamline the job search process, providing users with a simplified and organized experience. This platform aims to simplify the complexities of job hunting by offering a centralized hub for job seekers to manage their applications, connect with potential employers, and access valuable job search resources. By leveraging open-source technology, ReadyToTouch seeks to make job searching more accessible and efficient for individuals worldwide.
