## 📝 Summary

Dark Reader is an open-source browser extension available for Chrome and Firefox, designed to invert the colors of web pages to reduce eye strain and improve readability in low-light environments. This extension dynamically adjusts the color scheme of websites, making it easier to read and view content in the dark without compromising the original design intent. By automatically adapting to different web pages, Dark Reader provides a comfortable and immersive browsing experience.
