## 📝 Summary

LinuxCNC is an open‑source motion control system that turns a standard PC into a powerful controller for a wide range of CNC equipment, including mills, lathes, 3‑D printers, laser and plasma cutters, robotic arms, and hexapods. It provides real‑time trajectory planning, multi‑axis coordination, and a flexible configuration framework, enabling precise, repeatable operation of both hobbyist and industrial machines.
