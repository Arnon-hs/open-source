## 📝 Summary

VapourSynth is an open‑source video processing framework designed for clarity and ease of use. It provides a powerful, script‑driven environment where users can chain filters, manipulate frames, and perform complex editing tasks with a clean Python API. The project’s modular architecture makes it ideal for both simple cuts and advanced visual effects pipelines.
