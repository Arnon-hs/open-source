## 📝 Summary

jpki/myna is an open‑source utility for Japan’s My Number (個人番号) cards, providing tools to read, manage, and verify data stored on the cards and to perform JPKI (Japanese Public Key Infrastructure) digital signatures. It streamlines common tasks such as certificate extraction, signature creation, and verification, making it easier for developers and administrators to integrate My Number card functionality into their applications.
