## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project JuliaDSP/DSP.jl:

JuliaDSP/DSP.jl is an open-source digital signal processing library for the Julia programming language, providing a wide range of functionalities including filter design, periodograms, and window functions. This library offers a powerful and flexible toolkit for signal processing tasks, making it an ideal choice for researchers, developers, and engineers working with digital signals. With its extensive feature set and open-source nature, JuliaDSP/DSP.jl empowers users to efficiently process and analyze digital signals in a variety of applications.
