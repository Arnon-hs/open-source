Pumas is an open-source, user-friendly power usage monitor designed specifically for Apple Silicon devices. This tool provides users with detailed insights into their device's power consumption, empowering them to optimize their battery life and performance. By offering a transparent look into power usage, Pumas helps users make informed decisions about their device's settings and behavior.
