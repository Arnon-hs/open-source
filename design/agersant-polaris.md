## 📝 Summary

Polaris is an open‑source music streaming platform that lets users access and play their personal music libraries from any computer or mobile device. Built with a clean, responsive design, it provides a seamless, cross‑platform experience for browsing, searching, and streaming collections over a local network or the internet.
