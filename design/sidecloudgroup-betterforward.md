## 📝 Summary

BetterForward is an open‑source tool that enhances Telegram’s message‑forwarding capabilities, offering more control and flexibility when sharing content across chats. It provides a streamlined, user‑friendly interface for customizing forward behavior, making it easier to preserve context, media, and formatting. Ideal for developers and power users who need reliable, design‑focused forwarding solutions.
