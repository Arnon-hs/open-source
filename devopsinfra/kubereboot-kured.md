## 📝 Summary

Kured, a part of the Kubereboot project, is an open-source Kubernetes reboot daemon. It automates the process of upgrading and rebooting nodes in a Kubernetes cluster, ensuring a seamless and efficient upgrade experience. By handling reboots and upgrades, Kured simplifies the maintenance of large-scale Kubernetes deployments.
