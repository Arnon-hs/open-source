## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the Katenary open-source project:

Katenary is an open-source tool that automates the conversion of Docker and Podman compose files into a configurable Helm chart, streamlining the deployment process for Kubernetes applications. This project enables users to leverage their existing Docker and Podman workflows while taking advantage of Helm's powerful chart management features. By bridging the gap between containerization and orchestration, Katenary simplifies the transition to Kubernetes.
