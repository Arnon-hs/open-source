## 📝 Summary

Nelm is an open-source, Helm 4 alternative for Kubernetes deployment, designed to provide a modern solution for managing and deploying Helm Charts. It tackles long-standing issues with Helm and introduces new features to enhance the deployment experience. As a DevOps tool, Nelm aims to offer a more efficient and reliable way to manage Kubernetes applications.
