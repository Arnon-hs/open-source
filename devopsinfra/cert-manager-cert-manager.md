## 📝 Summary

Cert-manager is an open-source tool that streamlines the process of managing TLS certificates within Kubernetes environments. It automatically provisions and renewes certificates, ensuring a secure and scalable infrastructure. By integrating with Kubernetes, cert-manager simplifies the management of certificates, reducing the risk of certificate expiration and downtime.
