## 📝 Summary

The gocraft project is an open-source toolset designed to expedite the development of Go projects by providing pre-built templates and configurations for authentication, databases, gRPC services, Dockerization, Swagger documentation, and monitoring. This comprehensive framework aims to streamline the development process, allowing developers to focus on their core logic. By offering a range of essential functionalities out of the box, gocraft simplifies the creation and deployment of robust Go applications.
