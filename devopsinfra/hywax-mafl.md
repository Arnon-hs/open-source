## 📝 Summary

Hywax/mafl is a lightweight, highly customizable homepage generator designed for developers and product teams who need a clean, single‑page dashboard to display links, status widgets, and quick‑access tools. Built with minimal dependencies, it can be easily deployed in any DevOps or infrastructure environment—whether as a static site, a Docker container, or a serverless function—while offering flexible theming and extensible configuration through simple JSON/YAML files.
