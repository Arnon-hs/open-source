## 📝 Summary

khi is an open-source log viewer designed to facilitate Kubernetes troubleshooting. This tool enables users to efficiently navigate and analyze log data from their Kubernetes clusters, streamlining the process of identifying and resolving issues. By providing a centralized and accessible log viewing experience, khai helps DevOps professionals and operators quickly diagnose and respond to cluster problems.
