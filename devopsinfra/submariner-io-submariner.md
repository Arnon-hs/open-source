## 📝 Summary

Submariner is an open-source networking solution that enables seamless communication between Pods and Services across multiple Kubernetes clusters, facilitating hybrid and multi-cluster deployments. By providing a unified networking experience, Submariner eliminates the need for complex routing and tunneling configurations, making it easier to manage and scale distributed applications. With Submariner, organizations can create a more connected and agile cloud-native ecosystem.
