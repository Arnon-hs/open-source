## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the NasDock/AudioDock project:

AudioDock is an open-source, local multimedia player that combines music and audiobooks, allowing for seamless synchronization across multiple devices. This project enables users to access their audio library from anywhere, making it an ideal solution for those who value flexibility and convenience. By utilizing AudioDock, users can enjoy a rich audio experience on their devices without the need for cloud services.
