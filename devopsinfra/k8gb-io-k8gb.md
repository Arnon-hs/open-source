## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the k8gb project:

k8gb is an open-source, cloud-native Kubernetes Global Balancer designed to provide seamless load balancing and traffic management for distributed applications. This project enables efficient and scalable distribution of network traffic across Kubernetes clusters, ensuring high availability and responsiveness of applications. By leveraging Kubernetes' extensibility, k8gb offers a flexible and customizable solution for DevOps teams to optimize application performance and reliability.
