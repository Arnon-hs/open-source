## 📝 Summary

Opencost is an open-source project designed to monitor costs associated with Kubernetes workloads and cloud usage. This tool provides users with transparency and control over their expenses, enabling them to make data-driven decisions about their infrastructure and resource allocation. With opencost, users can effectively manage their cloud costs and optimize their resource utilization.
