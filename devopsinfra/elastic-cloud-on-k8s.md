## 📝 Summary

Elastic Cloud on Kubernetes enables users to deploy, manage, and scale Elasticsearch, Kibana, and other Elastic products on Kubernetes clusters with ease and efficiency. This open-source project provides a streamlined experience for operating and integrating Elastic products into containerized environments, leveraging the scalability and flexibility of Kubernetes. By simplifying the deployment process, it empowers users to focus on their data analytics and logging needs.
