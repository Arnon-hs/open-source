## 📝 Summary

Kyverno is an open-source policy engine that provides a unified framework for Policy as Code. It allows users to define and enforce policies across their Kubernetes clusters using a simple and declarative syntax, making it easier to automate and secure infrastructure configurations. With Kyverno, developers and security teams can ensure consistent and compliant environments across their entire organization.
