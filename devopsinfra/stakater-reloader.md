## 📝 Summary

Here is a 2-3 sentence summary for the stakater/Reloader project:

Reloader is an open-source Kubernetes controller that monitors ConfigMap and Secret updates, triggering rolling upgrades on associated Pods managed by Deployments, StatefulSets, DaemonSets, and DeploymentConfigs. This ensures seamless and automated updates to applications without requiring manual intervention. By automating the deployment process, Reloader simplifies DevOps workflows and enhances application availability.
