## 📝 Summary

The rabbitmq/cluster-operator is an open-source project that enables the management of RabbitMQ clusters within Kubernetes environments, streamlining their deployment, scaling, and maintenance. This operator provides a streamlined way to provision and manage RabbitMQ clusters, ensuring high availability, reliability, and scalability for distributed message queues. By leveraging Kubernetes, developers can more efficiently orchestrate RabbitMQ clusters.
