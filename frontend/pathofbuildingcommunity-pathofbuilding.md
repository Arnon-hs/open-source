## 📝 Summary

PathOfBuilding is an open-source, offline build planner for the action RPG Path of Exile. This tool allows players to plan and optimize their character builds without relying on online resources, providing a convenient and flexible way to experiment with different builds. By offering a comprehensive build planner, PathOfBuilding empowers players to make informed decisions and improve their gameplay experience.
