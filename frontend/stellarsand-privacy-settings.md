## 📝 Summary

The StellarSand/privacy-settings project is a comprehensive, open-source guide to navigating and optimizing the privacy settings of popular software and online services. This resource aims to empower users by providing clear, actionable information on how to protect their personal data and maintain online security. By categorizing settings by software and service, the project facilitates easy discovery and effective management of one's digital footprint.
