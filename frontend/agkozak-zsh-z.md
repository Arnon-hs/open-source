## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, zsh-z, is a native Zsh port of z.sh that enables users to quickly navigate to frequently visited directories. It offers a convenient way to revisit directories that have been accessed recently, enhancing productivity and streamlining directory navigation in the terminal. With its added features and native Zsh integration, zsh-z provides a seamless experience for users.
