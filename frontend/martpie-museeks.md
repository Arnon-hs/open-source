## 📝 Summary

Museeks is an open-source, cross-platform music player that provides a simple and clean user experience. This frontend application allows users to play, manage, and control their music library across various platforms, making it a versatile and accessible music player solution. With its minimalistic design, Museeks is perfect for those seeking a straightforward and efficient music listening experience.
