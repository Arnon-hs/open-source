## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the LaQuay/TDTChannels project:

LaQuay/TDTChannels is an open-source project that provides a comprehensive list of free and legal Spanish TV channels available on open TDT (Digital Terrestrial Television) broadcasts. This project offers users a free and accessible way to access Spanish TV channels without any subscription or payment requirements. The project is built as a frontend application, making it user-friendly and easy to navigate.
