## 📝 Summary

The recyclarr project is an open-source tool designed to automatically sync TRaSH Guides to your Sonarr and Radarr instances, providing a seamless and efficient way to manage your media library. This frontend application simplifies the process of keeping your guides up-to-date, allowing you to focus on enjoying your favorite shows and movies. By syncing TRaSH Guides, recyclarr helps ensure accurate and comprehensive metadata for your media collection.
