## 📝 Summary

The Speeduino project is an open-source, Arduino-based engine management system designed to provide a cost-effective and highly customizable solution for engine control. Utilizing the popular Arduino platform, Speeduino allows users to fine-tune their engine's performance and efficiency with ease. By leveraging the open-source nature of the project, users can contribute to and benefit from the ongoing development and improvement of the system.
