## 📝 Summary

AWX is an open-source project that provides a web-based interface, REST API, and task engine built on top of Ansible, enabling the automation of complex workflows and tasks. It serves as the upstream project for Red Hat Ansible Automation Platform, providing a comprehensive platform for Ansible users. With its robust features, AWX facilitates the efficient management and execution of Ansible playbooks and projects.
