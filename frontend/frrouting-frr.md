## 📝 Summary

FRRouting/frr is an open-source collection of routing protocols that provides a scalable and highly customizable suite of routing software. It supports various protocols, including BGP, OSPF, ISIS, and PIM, and is designed to meet the needs of large-scale networks and data centers. This software is ideal for organizations seeking a flexible and reliable routing solution.
