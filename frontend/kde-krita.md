## 📝 Summary

Krita is a free, open‑source digital painting application that runs on Windows, macOS, and Linux. Built on the KDE and Qt frameworks, it provides a comprehensive, end‑to‑end workflow for creating, editing, and exporting artwork—from sketching and line art to full‑color painting and final export. Its feature‑rich, user‑friendly interface makes it a popular choice for illustrators, concept artists, and hobbyists alike.
