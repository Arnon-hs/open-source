## 📝 Summary

Backstage is an open-source framework for building developer portals, providing a flexible and customizable platform for teams to create centralized hubs for their developers. It enables the creation of a self-service environment, where developers can access tools, documentation, and resources, streamlining their workflow and improving productivity. With Backstage, developers can build a tailored experience that meets their specific needs and integrates seamlessly with existing infrastructure.
