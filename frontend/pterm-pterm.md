## 📝 Summary

PTerm is an open-source, cross-platform Go module that simplifies console output by providing a range of customizable visualizations, including charts, progress bars, tables, and more. This versatile library offers a variety of interactive elements such as text input and select menus, making it a valuable tool for developers looking to enhance their command-line interfaces. With its 100% cross-platform compatibility, PTerm is an ideal choice for building modern and visually appealing console applications.
