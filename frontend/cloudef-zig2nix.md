## 📝 Summary

Cloudef/zig2nix is a Nix flake that streamlines the packaging, building, and execution of Zig projects. By providing ready‑made Nix expressions and development shells, it lets developers integrate Zig code into the Nix ecosystem with minimal configuration, making reproducible builds and dependency management straightforward. Suitable for both frontend tooling and general dev‑tool workflows.
