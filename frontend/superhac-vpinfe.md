## 📝 Summary

VPinFE is an open-source, cross-platform front-end designed for VPinball, a popular virtual pinball simulator. This project aims to provide a user-friendly interface for VPinball users, enhancing their overall experience on various operating systems. By leveraging an open-source approach, VPinFE encourages community involvement and continuous improvement.
