## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the kanri project:

Kanri is an open-source, offline Kanban board app designed for Windows, MacOS, and Linux. Built with simplicity and user experience in mind, this modern app utilizes Tauri and Nuxt to provide a seamless workflow. With its offline capabilities, Kanri allows users to stay productive and organized, even in areas with limited internet connectivity.
