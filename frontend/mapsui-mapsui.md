## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the Mapsui project:

Mapsui is an open-source, cross-platform .NET map component that supports a wide range of frameworks and platforms, including MAUI, Avalonia, Blazor, and more. It provides a unified mapping experience across various desktop and mobile environments. With Mapsui, developers can easily integrate interactive maps into their .NET applications.
