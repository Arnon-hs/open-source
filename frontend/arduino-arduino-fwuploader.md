## 📝 Summary

The **arduino-fwuploader** is a command‑line utility that streamlines firmware updates and SSL certificate provisioning for Arduino boards featuring WINC or NINA Wi‑Fi modules. It automates the flashing process, supports multiple board types, and integrates seamlessly into development workflows, making remote updates and secure communications easy for makers and developers.
