## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the AdguardTeam/dnsproxy project:

AdguardTeam/dnsproxy is an open-source, simple DNS proxy that supports various secure protocols such as DoH, DoT, DoQ, and DNSCrypt. This project provides a flexible solution for users to protect their online identity and maintain their DNS records safely. With its frontend-centric design, it offers an accessible way to manage and secure DNS queries.
