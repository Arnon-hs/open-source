## 📝 Summary

The krayin/laravel-crm project is an open-source, comprehensive Customer Relationship Management (CRM) solution built on Laravel, designed to cater to the needs of Small to Medium Enterprises (SMEs) and large-scale Enterprises. This free and open-source platform manages the entire customer lifecycle, providing an all-inclusive solution for customer management, sales, marketing, and support. It offers a robust and customizable framework for businesses to streamline their customer interactions and relationships.
