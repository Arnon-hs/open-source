## 📝 Summary

The tickrs project provides real-time ticker data directly in your terminal, allowing for quick and easy access to stock market information. This open-source tool is designed to bridge the gap between traditional trading platforms and the command line, catering to developers and traders who prefer a more streamlined interface. With tickrs, you can stay up-to-date on market movements in a straightforward and efficient manner.
