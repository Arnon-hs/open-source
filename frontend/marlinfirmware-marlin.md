## 📝 Summary

Marlin is an open-source firmware designed for RepRap 3D printers, supporting both 8 and 32-bit microcontrollers and various platforms. This versatile firmware is widely used in commercial 3D printers and can be easily customized for specific machines. With its comprehensive support, Marlin offers a reliable and efficient solution for 3D printing applications.
