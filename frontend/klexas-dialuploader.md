## 📝 Summary

The Dial-up-loader is an open-source project that recreates the nostalgic experience of a classic dial-up modem connection. This project simulates the process of connecting to an internet service provider through a terminal, complete with synthesized dial-up sounds. It's a unique blend of retro technology and modern software, available for anyone to explore and enjoy.
