## 📝 Summary

CliMA/Oceananigans.jl is an open-source Julia project that enables fast, efficient, and flexible ocean-flavored fluid dynamics simulations on both CPUs and GPUs. This software is designed to simplify the process of modeling complex oceanic phenomena, providing a friendly and user-friendly interface for researchers and developers. By leveraging Julia's performance and parallelization capabilities, CliMA/Oceananigans.jl accelerates ocean dynamics research and applications.
