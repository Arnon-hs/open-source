## 📝 Summary

Micro-Manager is an open-source project that integrates microscope control and image acquisition with the popular image processing software ImageJ. This allows researchers to automate and streamline their microscopy experiments, making it easier to collect high-quality images and data. By providing a user-friendly interface and advanced control features, Micro-Manager empowers scientists to optimize their imaging techniques and accelerate their research.
