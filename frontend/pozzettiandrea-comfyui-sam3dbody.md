## 📝 Summary

ComfyUI‑SAM3DBody is an open‑source wrapper that integrates the SAM‑3D‑Body model into the ComfyUI workflow, allowing users to easily generate and manipulate 3‑D human body representations within a visual node‑based interface. It streamlines the setup and execution of SAM‑3D‑Body’s capabilities, making advanced body segmentation and pose estimation accessible to developers and artists without requiring deep code modifications.
