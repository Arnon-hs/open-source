## 📝 Summary

Freesurfer is an open-source neuroimaging analysis and visualization suite that provides a comprehensive set of tools for processing, analyzing, and visualizing structural and functional brain images. This software is designed to support neuroscientists, researchers, and clinicians in their efforts to understand the complexities of brain structure and function, ultimately leading to better diagnosis and treatment of neurological and psychiatric disorders. With its robust features and user-friendly interface, Freesurfer has become a widely-used and respected tool in the field of neuroimaging research.
