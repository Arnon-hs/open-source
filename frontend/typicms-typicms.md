## 📝 Summary

TypiCMS is an open-source, modular, and multilingual content management system (CMS) built on top of the Laravel framework. This robust CMS allows developers to efficiently manage structured content such as pages, news, events, and more, providing a flexible foundation for diverse web applications. With TypiCMS, users can create and administer complex content with ease, streamlining their workflow and enhancing online presence.
