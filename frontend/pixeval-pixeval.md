## 📝 Summary

Pixeval is an open-source Pixiv client designed to provide a user-friendly interface for interacting with the popular Japanese online artwork community. This project allows users to browse, download, and manage Pixiv content from the comfort of their own application. It serves as a convenient alternative to the official Pixiv website or mobile apps.
