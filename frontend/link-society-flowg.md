## 📝 Summary

Flowg is an open-source, low-code log processing software that empowers users to create custom log processing workflows through a visual scripting interface. This user-friendly tool simplifies the complexities of log analysis, allowing developers and non-technical users alike to extract insights and valuable information from their log data. With Flowg, users can streamline their log processing tasks and enhance their data-driven decision-making capabilities.
