## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the ESPTimeCast project:

ESPTimeCast is an open-source, WiFi-connected LED matrix clock and weather display that provides users with real-time time synchronization and live weather updates. This compact design combines the functionality of a modern digital clock with a weather display, all accessible through a web-based interface. Built on ESP8266/ESP32 and MAX7219, ESPTimeCast offers a sleek and modern solution for home or office spaces.
