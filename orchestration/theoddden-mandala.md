## 📝 Summary

Mandala is an open‑source platform for orchestrating logistics workflows, offering a modular framework to coordinate tasks such as routing, inventory management, and shipment tracking across distributed systems. Built with extensibility in mind, it provides a declarative DSL, pluggable adapters for various transport APIs, and real‑time monitoring dashboards, enabling teams to automate complex supply‑chain operations without vendor lock‑in.
