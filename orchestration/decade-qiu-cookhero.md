## 📝 Summary

CookHero is an open‑source, multimodal platform that combines large language models, retrieval‑augmented generation, and agentic orchestration to deliver smart cooking assistance. It offers features such as AI‑powered recipe search, personalized meal planning, automated dietary logging, nutrition analysis, web‑search augmentation, and an extensible ReAct‑style agent/sub‑agent toolkit, enabling kitchen novices to become “cooking heroes.”
