## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the open-source project:

Feynman-Tutor is an AI teaching tool that utilizes the Feynman Technique to identify knowledge gaps by having the user teach the AI. This project allows users to work with popular LLM (Large Language Model) frameworks such as Claude Code, Cursor, Windsurf, and others. By actively teaching the AI, users can refine their understanding of complex topics and expose areas where they need further improvement.
