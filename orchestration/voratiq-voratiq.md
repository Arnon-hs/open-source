Voratiq is an open-source project that enables efficient workflow management by allowing users to run, delegate, and verify complex tasks before applying their outputs. It streamlines the process of orchestration, particularly useful in AI/ML applications, and serves as a valuable DevTool for developers. With Voratiq, users can maintain a high level of control and reliability in their workflows.
