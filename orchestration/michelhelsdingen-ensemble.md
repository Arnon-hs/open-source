## 📝 Summary

The Ensemble project is an open-source multi-agent collaboration engine that enables artificial intelligence (AI) agents to work together seamlessly as a single, cohesive unit. This AI orchestration platform allows agents to share knowledge, learn from each other, and make collective decisions, effectively creating a unified AI system. By facilitating collaboration between multiple AI agents, Ensemble aims to unlock new possibilities in AI applications.
