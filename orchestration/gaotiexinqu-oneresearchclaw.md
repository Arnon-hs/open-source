## 📝 Summary

OneResearchClaw is an open‑source, AI‑driven orchestration platform that automates the entire research workflow—from gathering raw materials to generating insights—using a skill‑based, self‑directed researcher agent. By combining modular “claws” for data acquisition, analysis, and synthesis, it enables fully autonomous, end‑to‑end research across diverse domains. The project is built on modern AI/ML techniques and is designed for easy integration and extensibility.
