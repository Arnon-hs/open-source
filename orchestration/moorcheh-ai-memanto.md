## 📝 Summary

Mémanto is an open‑source memory layer designed specifically for AI agents, providing fast, persistent, and query‑able storage of contextual information. It integrates seamlessly with orchestration frameworks, enabling agents to retrieve, update, and reason over past interactions to improve decision‑making and continuity. Ideal for developers building complex AI/ML pipelines that require reliable, scalable state management.
