## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the Micro-Agent project:

The Micro-Agent is an open-source, lightweight AI agent framework designed for vertical domain applications, providing a flexible and efficient solution for building intelligent systems. This framework enables developers to create and integrate AI models tailored to specific industry needs, promoting innovation and adaptability in various sectors. With its focus on frontend and orchestration, Micro-Agent facilitates seamless deployment and management of AI-powered applications.
