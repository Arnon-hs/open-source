## 📝 Summary

Moby Buildkit is an open-source, high-performance builder toolkit that enables concurrent, cache-efficient, and Dockerfile-agnostic building of container images. This versatile tool streamlines the process of creating images, making it suitable for a wide range of applications, including artificial intelligence, machine learning, and frontend development. By leveraging its powerful features, developers can speed up their build processes and improve overall efficiency.
