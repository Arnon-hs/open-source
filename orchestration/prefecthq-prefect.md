## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the Prefect project:

Prefect is an open-source workflow orchestration framework that enables users to build resilient and scalable data pipelines in Python. This framework simplifies the process of designing, executing, and managing complex data workflows, making it ideal for AI/ML and data-intensive applications. With Prefect, developers can streamline their data processing tasks and focus on high-level data science and engineering.
