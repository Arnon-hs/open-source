AxonFlow is an open-source project that provides a runtime control layer for production artificial intelligence (AI) systems. It enables the orchestration of AI models, allowing for efficient deployment, scaling, and management of complex AI workflows. By streamlining DevOps and infrastructure operations, AxonFlow aims to simplify the development and maintenance of AI-powered applications.
