JoySafeter is an open‑source enterprise AI Agent Platform that goes beyond simple chat interfaces, enabling developers to build, run, test, and trace autonomous agent teams through a visual orchestration dashboard. It integrates orchestration, AI/ML, and frontend capabilities to streamline the creation and management of collaborative AI agents for complex workflows.
