Here's a brief summary for the 'jacebrowning/gitman' project:

This tool allows users to easily install, update, and manage dependencies across various projects, providing a flexible and scalable solution for dependency management. By leveraging Git's version control capabilities, Gitman enables developers to streamline their dependency management workflow.
