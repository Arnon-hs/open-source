## 📝 Summary

**agent‑teams‑ai** lets you act as a virtual CTO, orchestrating a roster of autonomous AI agents that collaborate, exchange messages, and review each other’s output while you oversee a Kanban board and issue high‑level directives. The framework integrates with 75+ LLM providers (including Codex, Claude, and OpenCode) to let teams of agents handle end‑to‑end tasks, enabling you to build and run an AI‑first company with minimal manual coding. It combines orchestration, AI/ML, and a web‑based frontend for real‑time monitoring and control.
