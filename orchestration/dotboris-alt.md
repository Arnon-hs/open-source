## 📝 Summary

dotboris/alt is a lightweight version‑manager that lets developers quickly switch between multiple installed versions of command‑line tools. By providing simple commands to list, set, and toggle tool versions, it streamlines environment orchestration and reduces the friction of managing dependencies across projects. Ideal for DevOps and development workflows, alt integrates seamlessly with existing shells and CI pipelines.
