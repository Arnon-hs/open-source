## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the EmbeddedLLM/JamAIBase project:

JamAIBase is an open-source, collaborative spreadsheet for AI development, empowering teams to build, experiment, and iterate on AI applications in real-time. This innovative platform enables seamless collaboration by chaining cells into powerful pipelines, testing prompts and models, and evaluating LLM responses. By harnessing the power of JamAIBase, developers can streamline their AI workflows and accelerate innovation.
