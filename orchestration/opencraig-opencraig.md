## 📝 Summary

OpenCraig is a self‑hosted document‑intelligence platform that lets users query large collections of text while automatically providing citations to the exact source pages for every answer. Built with open‑source orchestration and AI/ML tools, it offers a cost‑effective way to ensure accurate, verifiable references without relying on proprietary services.
