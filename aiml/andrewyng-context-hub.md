## 📝 Summary

**Vibe Coding Works Until It Doesn't** is an open‑source dev‑tool that lets developers quickly prototype and iterate on AI‑powered features, but it also provides a fallback workflow for when the automated “vibe” suggestions fail. The project integrates with popular AI/ML libraries and offers clear guidelines for switching to manual coding or alternative models, ensuring productivity never stalls. It’s designed for engineers building intelligent products who need both rapid AI assistance and reliable manual control.
