## 📝 Summary

OneMore is an open‑source add‑in for Microsoft OneNote that enhances the note‑taking experience with a suite of lightweight yet powerful features, such as advanced formatting shortcuts, intelligent content insertion, and customizable workflow tools. Built with extensibility in mind, it leverages AI/ML techniques to provide context‑aware suggestions and automation, helping users create, organize, and retrieve information more efficiently within OneNote.
