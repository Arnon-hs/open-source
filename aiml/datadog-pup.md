## 📝 Summary

DataDog /pup is an open‑source command‑line companion that equips AI agents with a “Pup” capable of interacting across the Datadog ecosystem. It bundles more than 200 ready‑to‑use commands covering 33+ Datadog products, streamlining monitoring, observability, and automation tasks directly from the terminal. The project targets AI/ML developers and DevOps teams looking for a unified, scriptable interface to Datadog’s services.
