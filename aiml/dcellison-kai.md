## 📝 Summary

Kai is an open‑source, agentic AI coding assistant that operates through Telegram and runs locally on your machine. It integrates multiple persistent back‑ends for shell access, automated PR reviews, layered security, long‑term memory, and scheduled jobs, ensuring that all code, data, and execution stay under your control.
