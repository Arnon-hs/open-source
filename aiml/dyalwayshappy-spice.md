## 📝 Summary

Spice is an open‑source AI assistant that continuously perceives data from its environment and uses machine‑learning models to generate optimal recommendations or actions on your behalf—essentially acting as a “second brain.” By integrating multimodal perception with decision‑making algorithms, Spice aims to simplify complex choices and automate personalized workflows across a variety of domains. It is built for developers and users who want a ready‑to‑customize, intelligent decision‑support layer for their applications.
