## 📝 Summary

OpenOmniBot is an on‑device AI assistant designed for Android, enabling users to control terminals and execute a broad spectrum of tasks directly on their phones. Leveraging local machine‑learning models, it provides privacy‑preserving, real‑time assistance without relying on cloud services. The project combines AI/ML, frontend, and mobile development to deliver a versatile, extensible personal assistant for Android environments.
