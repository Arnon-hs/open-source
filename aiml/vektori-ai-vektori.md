## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the vektori-ai/vektori project:

Vektori is an open-source project that enables AI agents to remember and learn contextual stories, not just individual facts. It uses a three-layered sentence graph, comprising Facts, Episodes, and raw Sentences, all stored in a single database, and requires zero configuration. This innovative approach facilitates the development of more sophisticated and human-like AI capabilities.
