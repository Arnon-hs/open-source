## 📝 Summary

ArchiveBox is an open-source project that enables users to self-host web archiving capabilities. It can collect and preserve a wide range of digital content, including URLs, browser history, bookmarks, and social media bookmarks, and saves them in their original formats such as HTML, JavaScript, PDFs, and media files. This allows for a decentralized and private approach to web archiving and preservation.
