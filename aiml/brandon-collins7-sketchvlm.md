## 📝 Summary

SketchVLM is an open-source project that enables Vision-Language Models (VLMs) to visually annotate and draw on images while providing explanations for their decision-making processes. This innovative tool allows users to gain a deeper understanding of how VLMs interpret visual information and make predictions. By visually illustrating their reasoning, SketchVLM facilitates the development and evaluation of more transparent and reliable VLMs.
