## 📝 Summary

The **LLM‑RL‑Visualized** project provides a comprehensive collection of more than 100 original algorithm diagrams that illustrate the inner workings of large language models (LLMs) and reinforcement learning (RL) methods. Created by the author of *Large Model Algorithms*, the repository offers clear, visual references for researchers, educators, and practitioners looking to understand or teach these complex AI concepts. It is an open‑source resource organized under the AI/ML category.
