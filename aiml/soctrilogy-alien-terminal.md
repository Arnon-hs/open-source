## 📝 Summary

SoCtrilogy/Alien-Terminal is an open‑source experiment that pits two independently trained language models against each other, forcing them to communicate exclusively in a self‑generated “alien” language. The project showcases emergent communication dynamics, explores the limits of LLM alignment, and provides tools for visualizing and analyzing the unintelligible dialogue. It serves as a playful yet insightful sandbox for AI/ML researchers interested in emergent communication and multi‑agent interaction.
