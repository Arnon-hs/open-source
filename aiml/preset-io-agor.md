## 📝 Summary

preset-io/agor is an open‑source platform that lets teams run Claude, Codex, and Gemini AI sessions together on a shared, multiplayer canvas. It handles git worktrees, records and tracks AI‑driven conversations, and provides real‑time visualizations of each participant’s “agentic” contributions, making collaborative AI‑assisted development transparent and organized.
