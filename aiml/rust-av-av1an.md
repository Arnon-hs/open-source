## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the rust-av/Av1an project:

Av1an is an open-source, cross-platform command-line encoding framework that enables high-quality encoding of AV1, VP9, HEVC, and H264 video formats. It offers per-scene quality encoding, allowing for optimized video compression. This versatile tool is ideal for developers and DevOps professionals looking to streamline video encoding tasks in various applications and infrastructure setups.
