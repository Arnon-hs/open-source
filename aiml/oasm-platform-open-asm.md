## 📝 Summary

Open-ASM is an open‑source platform that helps organizations identify, monitor, and reduce their cyber attack surface through automated discovery and continuous asset profiling. Leveraging AI/ML techniques, it integrates with existing DevOps tools to provide real‑time visibility, risk scoring, and actionable remediation guidance across cloud, on‑premise, and hybrid environments.
