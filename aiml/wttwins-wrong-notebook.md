## 📝 Summary

Wrong‑Notebook (wttwins/wrong-notebook) is an open‑source AI‑powered “mistake notebook” designed for students to capture, analyze, and review their incorrect answers. Leveraging machine‑learning models, it automatically categorizes errors, provides targeted explanations, and suggests personalized practice to help learners close knowledge gaps. The project is aimed at enhancing self‑study efficiency through intelligent feedback and adaptive learning.
