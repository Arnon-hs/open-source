## 📝 Summary

gimli-rs/ddbug is an open‑source Rust library that provides lightweight utilities for capturing and displaying runtime debugging information, such as variable states, stack traces, and custom diagnostics. Designed with a focus on ease of integration, it offers macros and helper functions that format output clearly, making it useful for developers working on AI/ML projects who need quick insight into their code’s behavior.
