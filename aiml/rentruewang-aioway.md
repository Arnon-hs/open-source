## 📝 Summary

rentruewang/aioway is an open‑source framework that treats deep‑learning models as relational databases, letting users define, query, and manipulate neural networks through a declarative, SQL‑like interface. By leveraging RDBMS concepts, it aims to make AI pipelines more explainable, optimizable, and scalable while simplifying deployment across diverse environments.
