## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, "Legal Action Boundary Eval for agentic legal workflows," aims to define and evaluate the boundaries of legal actions in complex workflows. Its primary goal is to facilitate more effective and efficient legal decision-making by establishing clear guidelines for agent-to-agent interactions. By providing a framework for assessing legal boundaries, this project enables the development of more robust and scalable agentic legal systems.
