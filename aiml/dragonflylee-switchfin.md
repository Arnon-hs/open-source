## 📝 Summary

The switchfin project is an open-source, native Jellyfin client available on multiple platforms, including PC, PS4, PSVita, and Nintendo Switch. Its purpose is to provide users with a third-party alternative for accessing and managing Jellyfin media libraries on these devices. By utilizing a native client, switchfin aims to enhance the user experience and performance compared to web-based interfaces.
