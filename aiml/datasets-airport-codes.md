## 📝 Summary

The **datasets/airport-codes** repository provides a comprehensive, regularly updated collection of global airport identifiers, including IATA and ICAO codes, city and country locations, geographic coordinates, and additional metadata such as time zones and runway information. Designed for easy integration into AI/ML pipelines, data analysis, and mapping applications, the dataset is available in machine‑readable formats (CSV, JSON, etc.) under an open‑source license, making it a ready‑to‑use resource for developers and researchers working with aviation data.
