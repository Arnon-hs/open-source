## 📝 Summary

s-morgan-jeffries/apple-mail-mcp is an open‑source backend that adds an MCP (Message Control Protocol) server to Apple Mail, enabling users to manage their inbox through natural‑language commands powered by Claude Desktop’s AI capabilities. With this server, you can search, compose, send, and organize emails simply by describing what you want to do, letting the AI handle the underlying Mail operations. It bridges Apple Mail with conversational AI, making email workflows faster and more intuitive.
