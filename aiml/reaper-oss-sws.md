## 📝 Summary

The SWS extension is an open‑source add‑on for REAPER, the popular digital audio workstation from Cockos. It bundles a wide range of utilities, workflow enhancements, and scripting capabilities that integrate directly into REAPER’s UI, giving users advanced editing, automation, and project‑management tools. Although listed under AI/ML, the project focuses on extending REAPER’s functionality rather than implementing machine‑learning algorithms.
