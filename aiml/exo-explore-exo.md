## 📝 Summary

The exo-explorer project, also known as exo, is an open-source initiative aimed at running frontier artificial intelligence (AI) locally. This project allows users to execute and experiment with AI models directly on their machines, providing a self-contained environment for AI exploration and development. By running AI locally, users can gain greater control and understanding of their AI systems.
