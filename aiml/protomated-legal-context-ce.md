## 📝 Summary

Here's a 2-3 sentence summary for the protomated/legal-context-ce project:

LegalContext is an open-source Model Context Protocol (MCP) server designed to facilitate seamless communication between law firms' document management systems and AI assistants. This secure, standardized bridge enables law firms to leverage the power of AI assistants like Claude Desktop, while maintaining the integrity and security of their sensitive client data. By bridging this gap, LegalContext aims to streamline workflows and enhance collaboration between humans and AI in the legal industry.
