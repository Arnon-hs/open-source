## 📝 Summary

The open-data-registry is an open-source project on AWS that provides a centralized registry for publicly available datasets, making it easier for developers and researchers to discover and access these valuable resources. The registry enables the community to share, catalog, and discover a wide range of datasets, promoting data-driven innovation and collaboration. This initiative supports the advancement of AI/ML and data-driven projects by providing a single, accessible platform for dataset discovery.
