## 📝 Summary

### English

The **THREE.js‑PathTracing‑Renderer** is an open‑source library that adds real‑time path‑tracing with global illumination and progressive rendering to the Three.js WebGL framework. It enables physically‑based lighting and material effects directly in the browser, offering an interactive demo that showcases complex geometry rendered with high‑quality, noise‑reduced visuals. Ideal for developers and researchers needing fast, GPU‑accelerated photorealistic rendering in web applications.
