## 📝 Summary

The **FG‑CLIP** project presents a next‑generation CLIP model that delivers markedly improved fine‑grained discrimination across visual and textual modalities, achieving state‑of‑the‑art performance on benchmark tasks reported at ICML 2025 and ICML 2026. Built as an open‑source library, it offers pretrained weights, easy‑to‑use training scripts, and integration tools for developers and researchers seeking high‑precision multimodal embeddings.
