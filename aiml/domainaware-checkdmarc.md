## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the domainaware/checkdmarc project:

Checkdmarc is an open-source parser that simplifies the process of extracting and analyzing SPF and DMARC DNS records. This utility is designed to aid in the detection and mitigation of email spoofing threats by providing an easy-to-use interface for parsing DNS records. By utilizing checkdmarc, developers can integrate robust email authentication capabilities into their applications and services.
