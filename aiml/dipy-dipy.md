## 📝 Summary

DIPY is an open-source Python library providing a comprehensive framework for medical imaging analysis. It offers a wide range of tools for tasks such as spatial normalization, signal processing, machine learning, and statistical analysis, as well as specialized methods for diffusion, perfusion, and structural imaging applications. With DIPY, researchers and clinicians can efficiently visualize and analyze complex medical images in 3D and 4D+.
