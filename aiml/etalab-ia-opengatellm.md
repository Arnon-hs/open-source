## 📝 Summary

OpenGateLLM is an open-source, unified interface for running and managing self-hosted Large Language Models (LLMs). This project provides a centralized platform for users to easily deploy, monitor, and interact with their LLMs, streamlining AI development and deployment processes. By offering a standardized interface, OpenGateLLM aims to simplify the management of LLMs and foster greater collaboration within the AI community.
