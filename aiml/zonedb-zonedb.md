## 📝 Summary

The zonedb project is an open-source, publicly accessible database of geographic zones, aiming to provide a unified and standardized source of information for AI/ML applications and other data-driven projects. This database offers a comprehensive repository of zone data, facilitating the development of geographically aware systems and applications. By making zone data publicly available, zonedb aims to promote collaboration and innovation in fields that heavily rely on geographic information.
