## 📝 Summary

SJTUBeamer is an open‑source LaTeX Beamer template tailored for presentations at Shanghai Jiao Tong University. It offers a ready‑made slide theme that incorporates the university’s official colors, logos, and formatting guidelines, allowing users to create professional‑looking decks quickly. Although listed under AI/ML, the template is language‑agnostic and can be used for any academic or research presentation.
