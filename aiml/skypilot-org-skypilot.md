## 📝 Summary

Skypilot is an open‑source platform that simplifies running, managing, and scaling AI workloads across any compute environment—from Kubernetes clusters and Slurm schedulers to more than 20 public clouds and on‑premise resources. By providing a single, unified interface, it lets developers and ops teams provision, monitor, and orchestrate AI jobs without worrying about underlying infrastructure differences, accelerating experimentation and production deployment.
