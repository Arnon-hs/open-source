## 📝 Summary

Anil‑matcha/Open‑Lovart is a free, MIT‑licensed AI design agent that automates multi‑step creative workflows for posters, social graphics, brand kits, ad creatives, and video content. It serves as an open‑source alternative to commercial tools such as Lovart, Runway Agent, Luma Labs Agent, Krea Agent, Pika Agent, Galileo AI, and Magic Patterns, offering fully self‑hosted deployment. The project combines AI/ML techniques with design expertise to enable autonomous, high‑quality visual production.
