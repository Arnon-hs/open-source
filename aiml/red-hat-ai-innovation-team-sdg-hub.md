## 📝 Summary

The SDG Hub is an open-source Synthetic Data Generation Toolkit designed to support Large Language Models (LLMs). This project enables the creation of synthetic data, allowing developers to generate high-quality training data for their LLMs without relying on real-world datasets. By providing a scalable solution for synthetic data generation, the SDG Hub aims to promote more efficient and responsible AI development.
