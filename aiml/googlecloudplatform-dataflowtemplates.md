## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the GoogleCloudPlatform/DataflowTemplates project:

The GoogleCloudPlatform/DataflowTemplates project provides open-source, pre-built templates for Cloud Dataflow, enabling users to efficiently solve common in-cloud data tasks. These templates support a wide range of data processing needs, including data ingestion, transformation, and export, making it easier to integrate with other Google Cloud services. By leveraging these templates, developers can streamline data workflows and accelerate their data-driven applications.
