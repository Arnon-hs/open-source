## 📝 Summary

WUPHF is an open‑source collaborative platform that simulates a virtual office of AI “employees” which continuously build and maintain a shared knowledge base, ensuring they retain full context for any tasks you assign. It integrates multiple large‑language‑model back‑ends—including Claude Code, Codex, OpenClaw and locally hosted models via OpenCode—to provide flexible, code‑centric assistance across AI/ML and frontend workflows.
