## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the ChatLab project:

ChatLab is an open-source, local AI-powered analysis tool that helps you rediscover your social memories by reviewing your chat records. It utilizes AI technology to provide a personalized and private experience, allowing you to reflect on your conversations and interactions. This tool caters to users who value data privacy and want to explore their social memories in a secure and local environment.
