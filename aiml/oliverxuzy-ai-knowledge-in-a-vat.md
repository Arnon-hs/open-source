## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, "knowledge-in-a-vat", is an intelligent knowledge management system designed to integrate your local knowledge vault with Claude Desktop through the MidJourney Content API (MCP). It leverages AI/ML capabilities to facilitate seamless knowledge exchange and organization. By enabling this connection, users can access and manage their knowledge more efficiently.
