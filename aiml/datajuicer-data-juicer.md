## 📝 Summary

Data‑Juicer is an open‑source framework that streamlines the cleaning, filtering, and augmentation of large‑scale datasets for training and evaluating foundation models. It offers a modular, extensible pipeline—usable via both command‑line and a lightweight web UI—to apply customizable operations such as deduplication, quality scoring, and multimodal transformations across text, image, and audio data. The project targets AI/ML practitioners who need efficient, reproducible data‑preparation workflows for building robust foundation models.
