## 📝 Summary

jo-inc/pi-reflect is an open‑source tool that automatically reviews AI coding agent session transcripts, identifies recurring mistakes, and generates precise edits to the agents’ behavioral files. By learning from correction patterns, it continuously refines the agents’ code‑generation logic, reducing future errors and improving overall performance.
