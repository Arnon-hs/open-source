## 📝 Summary

Kompl is an open‑source knowledge compiler that aggregates scattered links, files, and bookmarks into a dynamic, self‑updating wiki. By ingesting new sources continuously, it builds a living knowledge base that grows richer with each addition, leveraging AI/ML for intelligent organization and offering both frontend and backend components for seamless integration.
