## 📝 Summary

This open-source project aims to find a cure for thalassemia, a genetic blood disorder affecting millions worldwide. The initiative, inspired by a personal appeal from a family member, seeks to bring together individuals and experts to collaborate on research and development of a life-changing treatment. By crowdsourcing knowledge and expertise, the project hopes to accelerate progress towards a thalassemia cure.
