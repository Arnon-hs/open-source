## 📝 Summary

Terraform is an open-source infrastructure-as-code tool that enables developers to create, modify, and manage infrastructure in a safe and predictable manner. It uses declarative configuration files to codify APIs, allowing for collaboration, version control, and editing of infrastructure like code. With Terraform, teams can efficiently manage their infrastructure and reduce the risk of human error.
