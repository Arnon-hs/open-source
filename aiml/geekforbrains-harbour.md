## 📝 Summary

Harbour is an open-source control plane designed to manage and orchestrate AI agents performing continuous tasks. It provides a framework for AI agents to operate in a coordinated and scalable manner, allowing for efficient and effective AI-driven workflows. By centralizing the management of AI agents, Harbour enables organizations to harness the power of AI for complex, ongoing tasks.
