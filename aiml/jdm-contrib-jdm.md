## 📝 Summary

The jdm-contrib/jdm project is an open-source directory providing direct links to delete your account from various web services. This initiative aims to simplify the often-complex process of account deactivation, promoting user data protection and online digital well-being. By aggregating these links in a centralized repository, jdm facilitates a more efficient and streamlined experience for individuals seeking to manage their online presence.
