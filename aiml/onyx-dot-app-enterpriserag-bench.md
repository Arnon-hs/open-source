## 📝 Summary

The **EnterpriseRAG‑Bench** project provides a curated dataset and a comprehensive benchmark suite for evaluating Retrieval‑Augmented Generation (RAG) systems on internal corporate documents. It enables researchers and developers to measure the effectiveness of RAG pipelines in handling proprietary, domain‑specific content, facilitating reproducible performance comparisons and driving improvements in enterprise‑focused AI applications.
