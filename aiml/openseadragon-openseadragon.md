## 📝 Summary

OpenSeaDragon is an open-source, web-based viewer for zoomable and pannable images, providing an interactive and immersive experience for users. Implemented in pure JavaScript, this library allows developers to easily integrate high-quality image viewers into their web applications. With OpenSeaDragon, users can zoom in and out, pan across images, and enjoy a seamless and responsive viewing experience.
