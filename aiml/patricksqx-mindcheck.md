## 📝 Summary

MindCheck is an open-source project that analyzes AI coding logs to detect over-delegation, a common issue in AI development where complex tasks are delegated to subtasks without sufficient oversight. This tool aims to help developers and researchers identify potential problems in their AI code and improve its reliability, efficiency, and transparency. By providing a more in-depth understanding of AI coding logs, MindCheck can facilitate the development of more robust and trustworthy AI systems.
