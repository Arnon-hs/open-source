## 📝 Summary

The Laravel AI SDK offers a clean, unified interface for integrating various AI services—such as OpenAI, Anthropic, Gemini, and others—directly into Laravel applications. By abstracting provider-specific details, it lets developers interact with multiple AI models through a single, expressive API, simplifying backend AI workflows and speeding up feature development.
