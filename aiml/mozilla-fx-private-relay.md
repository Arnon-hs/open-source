## 📝 Summary

The Mozilla Firefox Private Relay project is an open-source initiative designed to safeguard email users from hackers and trackers. It enables users to create disposable email aliases with a single click, protecting their primary email address from unwanted solicitations and malicious activities. By utilizing Private Relay, users can maintain a higher level of email security and anonymity.
