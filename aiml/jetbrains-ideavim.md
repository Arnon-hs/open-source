## 📝 Summary

IdeaVim is an open-source project that integrates the Vim editor into JetBrains Integrated Development Environments (IDEs). This project, known as JetBrains/ideavim, enables developers to utilize Vim's powerful editing features within their preferred JetBrains IDEs, enhancing productivity and workflow efficiency. The goal is to provide a seamless Vim experience within JetBrains environments.
