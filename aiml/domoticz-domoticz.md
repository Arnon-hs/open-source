## 📝 Summary

Domoticz is a free, open-source home automation system that supports a wide range of devices, including Z-Wave, Zigbee, and MQTT-enabled devices, with over 150+ compatible devices. This platform allows users to easily manage and control their smart home setup from a single interface. Domoticz is compatible with various operating systems, including Linux, Windows, and the Raspberry Pi.
