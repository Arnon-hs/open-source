## 📝 Summary

This open-source project involves benchmarking the proofreading abilities of Large Language Models (LLMs) in English, providing insights into their capacity for error detection and correction. By comparing the performance of different LLMs, the project aims to evaluate their potential as a tool for automated proofreading and editing. The results can help inform the development and application of LLMs in language-related tasks.
