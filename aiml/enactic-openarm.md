## 📝 Summary

enactic/openarm is an open‑source, fully articulated humanoid arm designed for physical AI research and real‑world deployment in contact‑rich environments. It provides a low‑cost, modular hardware platform with open firmware, sensor integration, and ROS‑compatible software, enabling researchers to experiment with manipulation, learning‑by‑demonstration, and reinforcement‑learning algorithms on a realistic robotic limb.
