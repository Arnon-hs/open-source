StandardEbooks /tools is an open‑source utility suite that streamlines the creation, formatting, and validation of Standard Ebooks’ high‑quality, DRM‑free ePub files. It automates tasks such as metadata insertion, CSS styling, image optimization, and compliance checks, enabling authors and volunteers to produce consistently styled, accessible e‑books with minimal manual effort.
