## 📝 Summary

The sbc-bench project is an open-source, simple benchmark designed to measure the performance of single board computers. It provides a standardized way to compare the capabilities of various SBCs, making it an invaluable tool for developers and researchers in the field of artificial intelligence and machine learning. This benchmark is essential for evaluating the suitability of SBCs for AI/ML applications.
