## 📝 Summary

### English

Open‑Less is an open‑source utility for macOS and Windows that lets you dictate text with a single hotkey: hold the key, speak, and release to insert AI‑polished, context‑aware content directly at the cursor in any application. It combines real‑time speech‑to‑text with large‑language‑model editing, providing seamless voice‑driven writing and refinement across the desktop.
