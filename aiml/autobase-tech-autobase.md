## 📝 Summary

autobase is an open‑source platform that automates the provisioning, scaling, and management of PostgreSQL® databases, effectively turning any infrastructure into a self‑service DBaaS. It combines AI‑driven monitoring and optimization with robust data‑layer tooling to simplify backups, migrations, and performance tuning for developers and ops teams.
