## 📝 Summary

The keras-hub project is an open-source platform that serves as a centralized repository for pre-trained Keras 3 models, making it easier for developers to access and utilize established AI/ML architectures. This hub offers a wide range of pre-trained models that can be directly integrated into various applications, saving time and effort in model development and training. By leveraging keras-hub, users can quickly tap into the collective knowledge and expertise of the community.
