## 📝 Summary

kbld is an open‑source DevTools utility that streamlines the process of building container images and pushing them to registries, integrating these steps directly into development and deployment pipelines. By automating image resolution, tagging, and upload, it removes manual Docker commands and ensures reproducible, version‑controlled images for Kubernetes‑based applications.
