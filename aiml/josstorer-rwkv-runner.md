## 📝 Summary

JosStorer’s **RWKV‑Runner** is a lightweight (≈8 MB) management and startup utility that fully automates the deployment of RWKV, an open‑source large language model usable for commercial purposes. It streamlines model initialization, provides a simple CLI/daemon, and exposes a compatible OpenAI‑style API, making it easy to integrate RWKV into existing AI/ML, backend, or development workflows.
