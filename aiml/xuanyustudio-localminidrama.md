## 📝 Summary

LocalMiniDrama is an open‑source, fully offline platform for creating AI‑generated short dramas and comics, handling the entire workflow from story conception to storyboard and final video. It runs locally so all data stays on the user’s machine, offering a highly flexible pipeline for AI‑driven live‑action or illustrated productions. The tool also includes a workflow management interface to streamline the creative process.
