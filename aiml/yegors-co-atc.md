## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the yegors/co-atc project:

The yegors/co-atc project is an open-source air traffic control simulator that utilizes local Software-Defined Radios (SDRs) to receive ADS-B telemetry and VHF communications. This simulator offers advanced capabilities, including optional AI-based functionalities, to enhance its realism and efficiency. It is suitable for developers and enthusiasts interested in aviation and AI applications.
