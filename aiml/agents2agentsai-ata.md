## 📝 Summary

Agents2AgentsAI (ata) is an open‑source framework that equips autonomous AI agents with the tools and workflows needed to tackle complex engineering and research challenges from problem definition through solution delivery. By integrating state‑of‑the‑art language models, domain‑specific toolkits, and orchestrated multi‑agent collaboration, the project enables end‑to‑end, reproducible problem solving for scientific and technical tasks.
