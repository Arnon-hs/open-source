## 📝 Summary

Elyra is an open‑source extension for JupyterLab that brings AI‑focused capabilities directly into the notebook environment, enabling data scientists and engineers to build, train, and deploy machine learning pipelines with ease. It integrates visual pipeline editors, AI model catalogues, and DevOps tools, streamlining the end‑to‑end workflow from experimentation to production. By combining Jupyter’s interactive experience with robust AI/ML and infrastructure automation features, Elyra helps teams accelerate development and operationalize AI solutions.
