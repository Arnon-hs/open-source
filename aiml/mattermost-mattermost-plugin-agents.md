## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the project:

The Mattermost Agents plugin is an open-source project that integrates multiple Large Language Models (LLMs) into the Mattermost platform, enabling users to leverage AI capabilities within their team communication. This plugin supports the integration of various LLMs, allowing users to access a range of AI-powered tools and features. By doing so, it enhances the functionality of the Mattermost platform and fosters collaboration between team members and AI systems.
