## 📝 Summary

MLE-bench is an open-source benchmark that evaluates the performance of AI agents in machine learning engineering tasks. It assesses the ability of these agents to effectively design, optimize, and deploy machine learning models, reflecting their practical skills in real-world scenarios. By providing a standardized evaluation framework, MLE-bench helps developers and researchers gauge the capabilities of AI agents in machine learning engineering.
