## 📝 Summary

Regent‑VCS is an open‑source version‑control system designed specifically for AI‑driven coding agents. It tracks, manages, and merges the code snippets and model‑generated edits produced by autonomous developers, enabling collaborative development, reproducibility, and conflict resolution across multiple AI agents. By integrating seamlessly with existing DevTools and AI/ML workflows, Regent‑VCS helps teams maintain a reliable code history while leveraging the productivity of AI assistants.
