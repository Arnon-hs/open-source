## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the MiniClaw project:

MiniClaw is an open-source, lightweight Large Language Model (LLM) agent designed for integration with Mini Micro. This tiny LLM agent enables developers to leverage AI capabilities in their projects, providing a compact and efficient solution for AI-driven applications. By harnessing the power of MiniClaw, developers can easily incorporate AI/ML functionality into their Mini Micro-based projects.
