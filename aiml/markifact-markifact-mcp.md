## 📝 Summary

Markifact‑MCP is an open‑source backend server that centralizes ad‑campaign management across major platforms—including Google Ads, Meta Ads, GA4, TikTok Ads, and LinkedIn Ads—while exposing a simple API for AI assistants such as Claude, ChatGPT, Gemini, Cursor, or any custom AI client. It supports the automation of over 300 ad‑operation workflows and incorporates a human‑in‑the‑loop verification step for every write operation, ensuring safe and accurate changes. This tool sits at the intersection of AI/ML, backend services, and developer tooling, enabling seamless, AI‑driven ad management.
