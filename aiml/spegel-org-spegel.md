## 📝 Summary

The spegel project is an open-source, stateless cluster local OCI (Open Container Initiative) registry mirror. It allows for a decentralized and efficient way to mirror container images, enabling teams to maintain control over their container registry and improve deployment resilience. By using spegel, organizations can ensure high availability and scalability of their container registry infrastructure.
