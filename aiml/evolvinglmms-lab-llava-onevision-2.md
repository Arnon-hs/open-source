## 📝 Summary

EvolvingLMMs‑Lab’s **LLaVA‑OneVision‑2** is a fully open‑source framework that streamlines the training of multimodal models capable of processing vision and language inputs in a unified architecture. By providing modular components, reproducible pipelines, and extensive documentation, it enables researchers and developers to democratize the creation, fine‑tuning, and evaluation of large multimodal models without proprietary constraints.
