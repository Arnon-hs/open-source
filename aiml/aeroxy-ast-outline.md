## 📝 Summary

aeroxy/ast-outline is a high‑performance, AST‑driven toolkit that extracts a concise “outline” of source files—signatures with line numbers, public APIs, and inter‑file dependency graphs—while omitting method bodies. It enables fast, symbol‑or behavior‑based search across a repository, making it ideal for both LLM‑powered coding agents and human developers. The project works across frontend and backend codebases and is positioned at the intersection of AI/ML and software engineering tooling.
