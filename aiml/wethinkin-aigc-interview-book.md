## 📝 Summary

WeThinkIn’s **AIGC‑Interview‑Book** is an open‑source compendium of interview and test‑preparation material for AI/ML engineering roles, especially those focused on AIGC, large language models, and AI agents. It aggregates core concepts, practical tips, and problem‑solving strategies across a wide spectrum of topics—including deep learning, computer vision, NLP, reinforcement learning, autonomous driving, big‑data mining, embodied intelligence, the metaverse, and AGI—to help candidates ace technical screenings and written assessments.
