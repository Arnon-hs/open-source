## 📝 Summary

The IBM/vakra project is an open-source benchmark designed to evaluate the performance of AI agents in multi-hop, multi-source tool-calling scenarios. This benchmark allows developers to assess the ability of AI agents to effectively integrate and utilize multiple tools to accomplish complex tasks. By providing a standardized evaluation framework, vakra enables the development of more robust and efficient AI agents.
