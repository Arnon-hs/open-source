## 📝 Summary

**stamparm/ipsum** provides a continuously updated feed of malicious IP addresses, enriched with blacklist hit scores that indicate how frequently each IP appears across various threat lists. By aggregating and normalizing data from multiple sources, the project helps security teams and AI/ML models quickly identify and prioritize high‑risk IPs for blocking or further investigation. It’s an open‑source resource aimed at improving threat intelligence and automated security workflows.
