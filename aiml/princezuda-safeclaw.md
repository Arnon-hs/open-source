## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

Safeclaw is an alternative AI-powered chat tool that offers a secure and natural way to interact with users through text and voice. Unlike other models, it allows users to opt out of language model usage, instead relying on intent and semantics to provide accurate responses. By default, Safeclaw provides a robust set of features, including text-to-speech (TTS) and voice-to-text capabilities, making it a versatile and effective solution for AI/ML projects.
