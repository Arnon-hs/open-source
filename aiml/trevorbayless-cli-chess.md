## 📝 Summary

trevorbayless/cli‑chess is a feature‑rich, terminal‑based chess client that lets you play both online via Lichess.org and offline against the powerful Fairy‑Stockfish engine. It supports every Lichess variant and offers extensive customization options for the interface and gameplay experience. The project combines AI/ML (through Stockfish), frontend (terminal UI), and backend components to deliver a seamless, cross‑platform chess platform.
