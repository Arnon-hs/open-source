## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, ophub/amlogic-s9xxx-openwrt, enables the installation and operation of OpenWrt on a wide range of devices powered by Amlogic, Allwinner, and Rockchip processors. It supports multiple models, including those from the S9xxx series, as well as several Allwinner and Rockchip chips. This project facilitates the transformation of these devices into customizable, open-source routers and network gateways for various AI and ML applications.
