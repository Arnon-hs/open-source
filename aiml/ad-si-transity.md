## 📝 Summary

Transity is a command‑line based plain‑text accounting tool that lets users record and monitor various resources—such as money, time, livestock, and beverages—directly from the terminal. By leveraging modern AI/ML techniques and a sleek frontend, it provides fast, searchable, and extensible bookkeeping for personal or small‑business finances. The project is open source and positioned as a future‑proof, developer‑friendly alternative to traditional accounting software.
