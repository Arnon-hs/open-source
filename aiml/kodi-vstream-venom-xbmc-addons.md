## 📝 Summary

Kodi‑vStream/venom‑xbmc‑addons is an open‑source add‑on that brings French streaming services to the Kodi (formerly XBMC) media center, allowing users to browse and watch a variety of French‑language TV channels, movies, and on‑demand content directly within Kodi. The project provides a collection of Python‑based plugins that integrate with popular French streaming platforms, handling authentication, playlist generation, and playback through Kodi’s built‑in video player. Although categorized under AI/ML, the add‑on primarily focuses on content aggregation and streaming rather than machine‑learning functionality.
