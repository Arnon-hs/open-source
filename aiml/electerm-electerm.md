## 📝 Summary

Electerm is an open-source, cross-platform client that provides a comprehensive suite of remote access and terminal tools. It supports various protocols such as SSH, SFTP, FTP, Telnet, serial port, RDP, VNC, and Spice, allowing users to access and manage remote devices from a single interface. Electerm is available for Linux, macOS, and Windows, making it a versatile solution for developers and IT professionals.
