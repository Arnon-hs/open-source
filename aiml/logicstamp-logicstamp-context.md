## 📝 Summary

The logicstamp-context project is an open-source Context Compiler for TypeScript, enabling the creation of deterministic, diffable architectural contracts and dependency graphs for AI coding workflows. This tool provides a robust framework for developers to manage complex dependencies and ensure consistency in their AI coding processes. By leveraging this compiler, developers can streamline their workflows and improve the overall efficiency of their AI development projects.
