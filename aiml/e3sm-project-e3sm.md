## 📝 Summary

The Energy Exascale Earth System Model (E3SM) is an open-source, high-performance model that simulates global climate and earth system processes. Developed to run on exascale computing systems, E3SM integrates advanced climate modeling, artificial intelligence, and machine learning techniques to improve our understanding of the Earth's complex systems. It is a valuable resource for researchers and scientists seeking to advance climate modeling and prediction capabilities.
