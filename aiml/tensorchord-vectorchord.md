## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the VectorChord project:

VectorChord is an open-source, scalable, and fast vector search system for Postgres that enables efficient similarity searches. It is designed to be disk-friendly, making it suitable for large-scale applications. As the successor to pgvecto.rs, VectorChord aims to provide a reliable and high-performance solution for AI/ML workloads and data-intensive applications.
