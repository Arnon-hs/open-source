## 📝 Summary

The huggingface/pytorch-image-models project is an open-source collection of PyTorch image encoders and backbones, providing training, evaluation, and inference scripts for various popular models such as ResNet, EfficientNet, and Vision Transformer. This comprehensive library includes pre-trained weights and allows for easy export of models for deployment. It offers a wide range of models for various computer vision tasks.
