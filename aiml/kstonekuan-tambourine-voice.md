## 📝 Summary

Tambourine-Voice is an open-source, AI-powered voice interface that enables users to dictate text naturally while working on any application. This personal voice assistant allows users to speak freely and have their words appear wherever their cursor is, offering full customization options for enhanced productivity. Developed as an alternative to Wispr Flow, Tambourine-Voice is a customizable solution for those seeking flexible and efficient voice dictation capabilities.
