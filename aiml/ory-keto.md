## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the ory/keto project:

Ory/keto is an open-source, cloud-native permission server that uses Google's scalable "Zanzibar" approach to provide a customizable and efficient solution for managing access control. This headless, API-first service supports various permission models, including ACL and RBAC, making it an ideal choice for developers seeking to modernize their permission systems. With its Go-based architecture, keto is designed to be highly scalable and secure.
