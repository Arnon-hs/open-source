## 📝 Summary

modu‑ai/cowork‑plugins is an open‑source collection of 21 Korean‑language plugins for Anthropic’s Claude, targeting domain‑specific expertise across B2B sectors such as finance, legal, HR, marketing, commerce, business intelligence, product management and sales. The suite bundles over 108 skills, including AI media generation (image, video, voice), office‑document handling (HWPX, DOCX, XLSX, PPTX, PDF) and HTML report rendering, making it a comprehensive Korean AI marketplace for enterprise workflows.
