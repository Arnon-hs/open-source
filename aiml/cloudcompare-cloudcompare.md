## 📝 Summary

CloudCompare is an open‑source 3D point cloud and mesh processing software that provides powerful tools for visualizing, editing, and analyzing large-scale spatial datasets. It supports a wide range of formats and includes features such as registration, segmentation, statistical analysis, and machine‑learning‑based classification, making it valuable for geospatial, industrial, and research applications.
