## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

NodeRSSBot is an open-source, Node.js-based Telegram RSS bot that allows users to subscribe to their favorite feeds and receive updates directly in the Telegram chat interface. This project aims to provide a flexible and customizable solution for users to stay up-to-date with the latest content from their favorite websites and blogs. Developed with AI/ML in mind, NodeRSSBot offers a cutting-edge approach to RSS feed management in the Telegram ecosystem.
