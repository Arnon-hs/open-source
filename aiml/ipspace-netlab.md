## 📝 Summary

ipspace/netlab is an open‑source framework that streamlines the creation and management of virtual networking labs, reducing the friction and complexity typically associated with such environments. By automating network topology provisioning, device configuration, and traffic simulation, it lets researchers, educators, and developers quickly prototype and test networking scenarios without cumbersome manual setup. The project leverages AI/ML‑friendly interfaces to enable data‑driven experimentation and analysis within realistic, scalable virtual networks.
