## 📝 Summary

canboat is an open‑source toolkit for working with marine NMEA 2000 and NMEA 0183 data. It includes a robust PGN decoder and utilities for reading and writing N2K messages, making it ideal for developers and researchers who need to explore, debug, or experiment with NMEA 2000 networks rather than serve as a finished end‑user application.
