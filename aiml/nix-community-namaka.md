## 📝 Summary

Namaka is an open-source snapshot testing framework for Nix, designed to help developers ensure the stability and correctness of their Nix expressions. Built on top of Haumea, Namaka enables comprehensive testing of Nix packages, making it easier to maintain and improve the reliability of Nix-based projects. This framework is particularly useful for AI/ML and DevTools projects.
