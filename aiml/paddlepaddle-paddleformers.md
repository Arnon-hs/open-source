## 📝 Summary

PaddleFormers is an open-source library of pre-trained large language models, built on top of PaddlePaddle, a popular deep learning framework. This library provides an easy-to-use interface for accessing a wide range of language models, enabling users to leverage the power of AI and machine learning in their applications. By using PaddleFormers, developers can quickly integrate language models into their projects and focus on building innovative solutions.
