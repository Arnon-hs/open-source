## 📝 Summary

twenty is an open‑source CRM that aims to replace Salesforce with a modern, AI‑first approach. Built with a sleek frontend and extensible design system, it lets teams manage customers, pipelines, and data while leveraging AI/ML for automation, insights, and predictive workflows. The platform is fully customizable, self‑hosted, and integrates seamlessly with existing tools, making it a powerful, privacy‑friendly alternative for businesses of any size.
