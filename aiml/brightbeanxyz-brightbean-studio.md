BrightBean Studio is an open‑source, self‑hosted social media management platform that lets users schedule, publish, and monitor content across more than ten major networks from one unified dashboard. Designed as a free alternative to tools like Buffer, Sendible, and SocialPilot, it combines AI‑enhanced features with a modern frontend and robust DevOps/infra capabilities for easy deployment and scaling.
