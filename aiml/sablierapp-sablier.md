## 📝 Summary

Sablier is an open‑source automation tool that launches containers only when they’re needed and shuts them down automatically after a period of inactivity, helping to reduce resource waste and costs. It works across a wide range of orchestration platforms—including Docker, Docker Swarm Mode, Podman, Kubernetes, and Proxmox LXC—making it a versatile solution for DevOps and infrastructure teams.
