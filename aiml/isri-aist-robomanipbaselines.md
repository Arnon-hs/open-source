## 📝 Summary

The RoboManipBaselines project is an open-source framework that combines various imitation learning techniques with benchmark environments for robotic manipulation. This platform allows researchers and developers to easily integrate and compare different algorithms for tasks such as grasping, manipulation, and task-oriented learning. By providing a unified environment for experimentation, RoboManipBaselines aims to accelerate advancements in robotic manipulation and AI-powered robotics.
