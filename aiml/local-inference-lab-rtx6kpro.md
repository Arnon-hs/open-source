## 📝 Summary

local-inference-lab/rtx6kpro is an open‑source guide and toolkit for running very large language models—such as Qwen 3.5‑397B, Kimi‑K2.5, and GLM‑5—on a single RTX 6000 Pro (or comparable PCIe GPUs) without relying on NVLink. It provides detailed instructions, configuration scripts, and performance‑tuning tips to enable efficient inference of multi‑hundred‑billion‑parameter models on high‑end PCIe GPUs, making massive LLMs accessible to researchers and developers with limited hardware interconnects.
