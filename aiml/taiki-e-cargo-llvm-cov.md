## 📝 Summary

taiki‑e’s **cargo‑llvm‑cov** is a lightweight Cargo subcommand that streamlines the use of LLVM’s source‑based code‑coverage instrumentation (`-C instrument-coverage`). It automatically builds your Rust project with the required LLVM flags, runs the test suite, and generates human‑readable coverage reports in formats such as HTML or LCOV, making coverage analysis fast and hassle‑free. The tool is open‑source, actively maintained, and fits naturally into typical Rust DevTools workflows.
