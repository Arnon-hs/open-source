## 📝 Summary

BoxLite is a lightweight compute substrate designed for AI agents, enabling developers to run sophisticated workloads locally on a laptop while seamlessly scaling to cloud resources when needed. It offers a flexible, elastic backend that balances performance with security, making it ideal for prototyping, testing, and deploying AI/ML applications at any scale.
