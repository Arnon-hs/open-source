## 📝 Summary

The AI Visibility Monitor is an open-source project that tracks whether a website is being cited or referenced by AI models such as GPT and Claude. This tool allows users to monitor and stay informed about the presence and influence of their site's content in the world of AI. By using this project, developers and content creators can gain insight into the reach and impact of their online work.
