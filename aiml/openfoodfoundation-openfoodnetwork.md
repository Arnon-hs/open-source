## 📝 Summary

The Open Food Network is an open-source platform that connects local food suppliers, distributors, and consumers to facilitate the trade of fresh produce. This project utilizes AI/ML capabilities to streamline the process of buying and selling local food, ultimately supporting sustainable food systems and local economies. By promoting transparency and cooperation, the Open Food Network aims to create a more equitable and resilient food network.
