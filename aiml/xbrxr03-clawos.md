## 📝 Summary

Clawos is an open-source, self-hosted runtime environment for local AI agents, providing a standardized platform for developing and running AI applications. Built on OpenClaw and Ollama, Clawos offers memory management, tools, and permission control to ensure secure and efficient AI operations. This comprehensive framework simplifies the development and deployment of AI models, making it an ideal choice for developers and researchers.
