## 📝 Summary

Awesome‑Chinese‑LLM is a curated collection of open‑source Chinese large language models that prioritize smaller scale, low training cost, and private deployment. It organizes foundational models, domain‑specific fine‑tuned versions, associated datasets, and tutorials, providing a one‑stop resource for developers and researchers working with Chinese LLMs.
