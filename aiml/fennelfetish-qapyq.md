## 📝 Summary

FennelFetish/qapyq is an open-source AI-assisted media curator that streamlines the process of captioning, cropping, and masking large image and video datasets. This tool is specifically designed to optimize workflows for LoRA (Large Model) and diffusion training, making it a valuable resource for developers and researchers in the AI and data science communities. By automating these tasks, qapyq enables users to efficiently prepare their datasets for training and testing AI models.
