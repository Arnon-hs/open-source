## 📝 Summary

SwarmAI is an open‑source personal AI command center that operates around the clock, acting as a collaborative “AI team” for users. It maintains contextual memory across sessions, continuously learns from interactions, and builds upon previous insights to deliver increasingly valuable assistance. Ideal for developers, researchers, and power users who need a persistent, self‑improving AI companion.
