## 📝 Summary

PentHertz /LUKSbox is a Rust‑based encrypted‑container utility that lets you securely store sensitive files on cloud services or shared media without having to trust the host system. It supports a wide range of authentication methods—including passphrase, FIDO2 devices (YubiKey, Titan, Nitrokey, Windows Hello), TPM 2.0, and hybrid post‑quantum keyslots (ML‑KEM‑768/1024)—and can be mounted as a native drive on Linux, macOS, and Windows.
