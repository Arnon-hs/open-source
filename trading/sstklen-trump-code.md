## 📝 Summary

s​stklen’s **trump‑code** project leverages a massive collection of 31.5 million AI models to decode the content of former President Donald Trump’s social‑media posts and translate their sentiment into actionable signals for U.S. equity markets. By combining multilingual natural‑language processing with quantitative trading heuristics, the open‑source toolkit achieves an approximate 61 % hit rate when predicting short‑term stock movements based on Trump‑related commentary. The repository provides the full model pipeline, data preprocessing scripts, and back‑testing utilities for researchers and traders interested in AI‑driven sentiment analysis.
