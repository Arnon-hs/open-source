## 📝 Summary

KylinMountain/TradingAgents‑AShare is an open‑source, multi‑agent research platform for China’s A‑share market. It leverages the TradingAgents framework to simulate 15 AI agents that collaborate and debate in real time, providing end‑to‑end visualized workflows and integration with OpenClaw and Claude Code, while offering a one‑click Docker deployment for easy setup.
