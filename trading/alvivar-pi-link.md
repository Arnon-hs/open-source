Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

This decentralized network allows terminals to automatically discover each other, exchange messages, and orchestrate tasks, streamlining collaboration and workflow across agents on localhost. Suitable for applications in trading and AI/ML, pi-link empowers seamless inter-terminal communication."
