## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the open-source project:

This loop combines structured Product Requirement Definition (PRD) generation, dependency graph execution, and review gates to ensure seamless and efficient development. By incorporating Iron Law verification, quantum-loop aims to enhance the overall development process and maintain high-quality code.
