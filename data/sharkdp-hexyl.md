## 📝 Summary

The sharkdp/hexyl project is an open-source command-line hex viewer designed to provide a simple and intuitive interface for examining binary data. It allows users to easily navigate and display hexadecimal representations of files, along with their corresponding ASCII characters and byte offsets. This tool is particularly useful for debugging and troubleshooting binary data in various formats.
