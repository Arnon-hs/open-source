## 📝 Summary

Timescaledb is an open-source time-series database that leverages Postgres to provide high-performance real-time analytics. This Postgres extension is specifically designed to handle large volumes of time-stamped data, ideal for applications requiring fast data ingestion and querying. By utilizing timescaledb, users can efficiently store and analyze large amounts of data from various sources.
