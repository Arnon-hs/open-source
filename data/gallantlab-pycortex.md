## 📝 Summary

Pycortex is an open‑source Python toolkit designed for visualizing functional MRI data on cortical surface models. It provides a flexible framework for mapping, rendering, and interacting with surface‑based neuroimaging results, making it easier to explore and present brain activity patterns. The project is maintained under the “Data” category and is freely available for community contributions.
