## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the Bearer project:

Bearer is an open-source static application security testing (SAST) tool designed to identify, categorize, and prioritize potential security and privacy vulnerabilities in code. This comprehensive scanning tool helps developers and organizations discover and address risks, ensuring the integrity and confidentiality of their data. By utilizing Bearer, developers can strengthen their applications against various security threats and maintain a secure coding practice.
