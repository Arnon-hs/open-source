## 📝 Summary

etcd is an open-source, distributed key-value store that provides a reliable and fault-tolerant solution for storing critical data in distributed systems. It enables users to maintain consistency and high availability across multiple nodes, making it an essential component for various use cases, including container orchestration and microservices architecture. With etcd, developers can ensure the reliability and scalability of their distributed systems.
