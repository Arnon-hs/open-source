## 📝 Summary

CrateDB is an open-source, distributed SQL database designed for handling massive amounts of data in real-time, even with complex queries. Built on top of Lucene and PostgreSQL-compatible, CrateDB provides a scalable solution for storing and analyzing data. It enables users to process and analyze large datasets efficiently, making it suitable for various data-intensive applications.
