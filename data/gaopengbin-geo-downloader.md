## 📝 Summary

GeoDownloader is a cross‑platform desktop application (Windows, macOS, Linux) that simplifies the acquisition of geospatial imagery. It lets users search for and download GeoTIFF tiles, Google 3D Tiles, and Esri Wayback imagery directly to their machine, handling large datasets and coordinate conversions automatically. The open‑source tool is geared toward GIS professionals and hobbyists needing fast, offline access to a variety of map layers.
