## 📝 Summary

Telemetry‑Parser is an open‑source utility that extracts and decodes real‑time metadata from video recordings and other telemetry sources. It supports a range of formats—including Sony, GoPro GPMF, Insta360, and Betaflight Blackbox (both CSV and binary)—allowing users to parse, analyze, and repurpose embedded sensor data for further processing.
