## 📝 Summary

The IQSS Dataverse is an open-source research data repository software that enables researchers to share, cite, and manage their data in a robust and secure environment. This platform facilitates data sharing and collaboration, allowing researchers to preserve their data for the long term and ensure its reproducibility. By using Dataverse, researchers can increase the visibility and impact of their work while promoting transparency and accountability in the research process.
