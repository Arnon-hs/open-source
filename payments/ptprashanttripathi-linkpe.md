## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the Linkpe project:

Linkpe is an open-source Node.js API that enables the generation of shareable UPI payment links, streamlining the payment process for users. This project combines the power of Node.js and AI/ML capabilities to provide a seamless payment experience. By utilizing Linkpe, developers can easily integrate UPI payments into their applications.
