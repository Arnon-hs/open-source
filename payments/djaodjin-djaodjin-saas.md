## 📝 Summary

djaodjin‑saas is an open‑source Django framework that streamlines the creation and management of software‑as‑a‑service (SaaS) platforms. It provides out‑of‑the‑box features for subscription billing, payment processing, plan catalogues, and user‑level access control, allowing businesses to launch and scale recurring‑revenue products quickly. The modular design makes it easy to integrate with existing Django projects and customize workflows for diverse SaaS models.
