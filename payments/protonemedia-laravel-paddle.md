## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, laravel-paddle, is a Laravel package that integrates with Paddle.com, a payment gateway, to simplify online transactions. By utilizing this package, developers can easily manage Paddle.com APIs and webhooks within their Laravel applications, streamlining the payment process. It's particularly useful for backend developers looking to enhance their platform's e-commerce capabilities.
