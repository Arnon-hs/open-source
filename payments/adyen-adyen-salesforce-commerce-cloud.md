## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, Adyen/adyen-salesforce-commerce-cloud, is an integration of Adyen's payment services with Salesforce Commerce Cloud, enabling seamless payment processing for online retailers. It allows developers to easily integrate Adyen's payment capabilities into their Salesforce Commerce Cloud-based e-commerce platforms. This integration simplifies payment management and enhances the overall shopping experience for customers.
