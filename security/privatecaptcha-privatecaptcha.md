## 📝 Summary

PrivateCaptcha is an open-source, self-hostable CAPTCHA service that prioritizes user privacy, developed and hosted in the European Union. This project provides an independent and reliable way to verify users without compromising their data, making it an attractive alternative to traditional CAPTCHA services. By hosting it locally, users can maintain full control over their data and ensure compliance with EU data protection regulations.
