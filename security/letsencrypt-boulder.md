## 📝 Summary

Boulder is an open-source, ACME-based certificate authority written in Go. It provides a scalable and secure solution for issuing and managing digital certificates. As a critical component of the Let's Encrypt project, Boulder enables automatic certificate issuance and renewal for secure websites and applications.
