## 📝 Summary

NeverIdle is an open‑source utility that periodically generates lightweight resource usage to keep Oracle (甲骨文) services alive, preventing them from being automatically shut down due to inactivity. By scheduling regular “waste” operations, it ensures continuous availability of Oracle instances without requiring manual intervention. The tool is lightweight, configurable, and suited for DevOps workflows that need persistent Oracle connections.
