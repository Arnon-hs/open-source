## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary for the open-source project:

TFLint-ruleset-azurerm is an open-source ruleset for TFLint, a Terraform linter that helps catch errors and enforce best practices in your infrastructure code. This ruleset specifically targets the Terraform provider for Azure, ensuring that your Azure infrastructure configurations adhere to industry standards and best practices. By integrating this ruleset into your development workflow, you can catch and fix potential issues before provisioning your infrastructure.
