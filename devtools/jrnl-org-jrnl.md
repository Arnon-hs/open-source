## 📝 Summary

jrnl is a command-line note-taking application that allows users to collect their thoughts and notes without leaving the terminal. This open-source project provides a simple and efficient way to jot down notes, thoughts, or ideas, making it an ideal tool for developers, programmers, and anyone who prefers a command-line interface. jrnl supports various export options and can be easily integrated into daily workflows.
