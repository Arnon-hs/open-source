## 📝 Summary

Flagger is an open-source Kubernetes operator that enables progressive delivery strategies, including Canary, A/B Testing, and Blue/Green deployments. It automates the rollout and rollback of deployments, allowing developers to safely and efficiently deploy new versions of their applications. With Flagger, teams can adopt continuous experimentation and deployment practices, reducing the risk of disruptions and improving the overall quality of their applications.
