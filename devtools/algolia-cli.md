Algolia's official CLI (Command-Line Interface) tool is a developer-friendly, open-source utility for simplifying interactions with the Algolia platform. It enables users to effortlessly manage and configure their Algolia instances, indices, and settings from the comfort of their terminal. With this CLI, developers can streamline their search infrastructure setup and maintenance tasks.
