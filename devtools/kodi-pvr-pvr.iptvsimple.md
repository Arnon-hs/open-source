## 📝 Summary

Here is a brief summary of the open-source project:

The IPTV Simple client is an open-source project that enables users to integrate IPTV channels into their Kodi media player as a PVR (Personal Video Recorder) device. This project provides a simple and straightforward way to manage and access IPTV content within Kodi, enhancing the overall user experience. By leveraging this plugin, Kodi users can easily stream and record IPTV channels.
