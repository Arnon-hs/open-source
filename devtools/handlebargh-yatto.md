## 📝 Summary

Yatto is an interactive, command-line todo-list that utilizes version control to track changes. This feature-rich application allows users to easily manage tasks, collaborate with others, and maintain a complete history of task modifications. With Yatto, users can efficiently organize and prioritize tasks in a structured and accessible way.
