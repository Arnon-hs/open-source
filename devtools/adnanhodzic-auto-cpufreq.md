## 📝 Summary

Auto‑cpufreq is a lightweight, open‑source daemon for Linux that continuously monitors system load and dynamically adjusts CPU frequency and power settings to achieve optimal performance while minimizing energy consumption. It supports a wide range of hardware, integrates with systemd, and offers configurable policies for users who want automatic, hassle‑free CPU scaling without manual tuning.
