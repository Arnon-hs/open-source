## 📝 Summary

The Pachli Android app is an open-source project that provides a mobile interface for interacting with Pachli, a distributed computing platform. This app allows users to manage and monitor their Pachli clusters, submit tasks, and track their progress on-the-go. By providing a mobile-friendly interface, users can now easily access and utilize the power of Pachli from their Android devices.
