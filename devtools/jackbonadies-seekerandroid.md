## 📝 Summary

SeekerAndroid is an open-source Android client for the Soulseek peer-to-peer network, allowing users to access and share files with others on the platform. This mobile application provides a user-friendly interface for discovering and downloading content from the Soulseek community. With SeekerAndroid, users can enjoy a seamless and portable experience on their Android devices.
