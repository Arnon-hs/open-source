## 📝 Summary

**poetaman/arttime** is an open‑source command‑line tool that turns the terminal into a visual timekeeper by rendering clocks, timers, and pattern‑based schedules as ASCII or text art. It combines aesthetic, customizable artwork with practical time‑management functions, making it a handy utility for developers and power users who appreciate both form and function.
