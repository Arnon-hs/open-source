## 📝 Summary

Phobos is an open‑source extension for the Ares mod of the classic real‑time strategy game *Command & Conquer: Red Alert 2 – Yuri’s Revenge*. It enhances the original engine with additional scripting, gameplay mechanics, and developer tools, allowing modders to create richer maps, units, and custom content while maintaining full compatibility with existing Ares features.
