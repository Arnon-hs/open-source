## 📝 Summary

**dip** is a command‑line development tool that lets you interact with Dockerized applications as if they were running natively on your machine. It streamlines typical dev‑ops tasks—such as building, running, and debugging containers—by providing a simple, unified CLI interface. Ideal for developers who want the convenience of local tooling while leveraging Docker’s isolation and reproducibility.
