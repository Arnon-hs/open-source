## 📝 Summary

Django CMS is an open-source enterprise Content Management System (CMS) built on the Django framework, providing a developer-friendly platform for creating and managing complex websites and applications. Its ease of use makes it an ideal choice for both developers and non-technical users, allowing for rapid development and deployment of custom content and features. With Django CMS, users can create, manage, and publish content with flexibility and scalability.
