Waboodoo/HTTP-Shortcuts is an open-source Android application that empowers users to create custom home screen shortcuts that can trigger a wide range of actions, including arbitrary HTTP requests. This versatile tool allows users to streamline their workflow and automate tasks by providing a simple and intuitive way to create custom shortcuts. With Waboodoo, users can take control of their Android experience and tailor it to their specific needs.
