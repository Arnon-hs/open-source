## 📝 Summary

This open-source project, "deep_causality", is a Rust library implementing hyper-geometric computational causality, a method for analyzing complex causal relationships in data. It is designed to provide a robust and efficient framework for identifying causal interactions and understanding the underlying mechanisms driving system behavior. By leveraging Rust's performance and safety features, this library aims to accelerate research and development in the field of computational causality.
