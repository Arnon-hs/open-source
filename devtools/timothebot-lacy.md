## 📝 Summary

timothebot’s **lacy** is a lightweight, high‑performance alternative to the classic `cd` command, designed specifically for users who navigate the filesystem through a terminal with a “lacy” (smooth, fluid) workflow. It instantly resolves and jumps to directories using fuzzy matching and configurable shortcuts, making directory traversal fast and magical without sacrificing the simplicity of standard shell navigation. Suitable for developers and power users looking for a snappy, customizable CLI navigation tool.
