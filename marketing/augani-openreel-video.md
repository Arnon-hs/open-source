## 📝 Summary

OpenReel Video is a fully browser‑based, open‑source video editor that offers professional‑grade editing features without requiring any installation, cloud uploads, or watermarks. Designed as a free alternative to CapCut, it lets users create, trim, and enhance videos directly in their web browser, making it ideal for marketers and creators who need a quick, privacy‑friendly editing solution.
