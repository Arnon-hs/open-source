## 📝 Summary

The Cramino project is an open-source tool designed for fast quality evaluation of BAM/CRAM files, particularly optimized for long-read data. This project enables researchers and scientists to quickly assess the integrity and reliability of their long-read sequencing data, facilitating more efficient analysis and decision-making in genomics research. With its focus on speed and performance, Cramino serves as a valuable resource for those working with long-read data.
