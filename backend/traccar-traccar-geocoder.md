## 📝 Summary

The traccar/traccar-geocoder is an open-source reverse geocoding service, designed to provide location-based information and mapping functionality. This service enables users to convert geographical coordinates into human-readable addresses, enhancing the accuracy and usability of location data. It is a backend component of the Traccar project, offering a scalable and customizable solution for geospatial applications.
