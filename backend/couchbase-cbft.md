## 📝 Summary

Couchbase Full Text (CBFT) is an open-source, high-performance search engine designed for efficient text search and retrieval. It enables developers to build scalable and flexible search capabilities within their applications, leveraging the power of full-text indexing and querying. By integrating CBFT, developers can enhance user experiences with fast and accurate search results.
