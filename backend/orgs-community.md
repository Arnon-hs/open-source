## 📝 Summary

This open‑source project documents the recent, undocumented change to GitHub’s PushEvent webhook payload where the `commits` array was removed, causing downstream tools to break. It explains what broke, provides a quick mitigation strategy, and offers a reusable library that normalises incoming webhook data and alerts developers to future payload changes.
