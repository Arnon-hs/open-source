## 📝 Summary

The Viam Server, also known as the Robotic Development Kit (RDK), is an open-source backend platform designed to simplify the development, deployment, and management of robotic applications. It offers a scalable and secure infrastructure for integrating hardware, software, and data, facilitating the creation of complex robotic systems. This platform empowers developers to build and integrate custom robotics solutions with ease.
