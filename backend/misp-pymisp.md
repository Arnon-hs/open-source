## 📝 Summary

MISP/PyMISP is an open-source Python library that facilitates interaction with the MISP (Malware Information Sharing Platform) Rest API. This library enables developers to easily automate and integrate MISP features into their applications, promoting efficient threat intelligence sharing and management. By leveraging PyMISP, users can streamline their MISP workflows and enhance their overall cybersecurity posture.
