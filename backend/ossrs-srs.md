## 📝 Summary

Here's a brief summary of the ossrs/srs project:

SRS (Simple Real-Time Server) is a highly efficient, open-source media server supporting a wide range of protocols and codecs. It enables real-time streaming of video and audio content over various networks and platforms, including RTMP, WebRTC, HLS, and others. With support for multiple codecs such as H.264, VP9, and AAC, SRS is a versatile solution for streaming and broadcasting applications.
