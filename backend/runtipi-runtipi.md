## 📝 Summary

Runtipi is an open-source homeserver project that simplifies self-hosting by offering a one-command setup and one-click installations for popular self-hosted applications. This user-friendly platform aims to make it easy for everyone to host their favorite apps, reducing the technical complexity often associated with self-hosting. By leveraging a streamlined installation process, Runtipi empowers individuals to take control of their digital lives and data.
