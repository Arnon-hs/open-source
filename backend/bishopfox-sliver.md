## 📝 Summary

BishopFox/sliver is an open-source adversarial emulation framework designed to mimic the behavior of real-world threat actors. This framework enables security professionals to simulate attacks, test defenses, and improve their incident response capabilities in a controlled environment. By using sliver, organizations can better prepare for and respond to potential cyber threats.
