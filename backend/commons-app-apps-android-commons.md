## 📝 Summary

The Wikimedia Commons Android app, an open-source project hosted on common-app/apps-android-commons, enables users to conveniently upload photos from their Android devices to the Wikimedia Commons platform. This mobile application streamlines the process of sharing high-quality images with the global Wikimedia community. By leveraging this app, users can contribute to the growth of the Commons repository with their own digital content.
