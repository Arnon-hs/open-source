## 📝 Summary

Repology‑Updater is the backend service that powers the Repology data aggregation platform. It continuously synchronizes and normalizes package information from a wide variety of software repositories, ensuring that the central database stays current and consistent. Designed as a robust, open‑source component, it handles large‑scale data fetching, parsing, and storage for downstream analytics and web interfaces.
