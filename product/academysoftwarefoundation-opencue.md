## 📝 Summary

The OpenCue project, developed by the Academy Software Foundation, is an open-source render management system designed for visual effects and animation productions. It allows users to manage, schedule, and monitor large-scale rendering tasks, streamlining the workflow and improving overall efficiency. With OpenCue, studios can deploy a customizable and scalable solution for their rendering needs, reducing costs and enhancing productivity.
