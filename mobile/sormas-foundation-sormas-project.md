## 📝 Summary

SORMAS (Surveillance, Outbreak Response Management and Analysis System) is an open‑source, mobile‑first platform that provides real‑time early warning, case tracking, and outbreak response tools for infectious disease management. It enables health authorities to collect, analyze, and share epidemiological data across regions, supporting rapid decision‑making and coordinated interventions. The project is community‑driven and freely extensible for diverse public‑health settings.
