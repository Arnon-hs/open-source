## 📝 Summary

The MusicRecognizer app is an open-source Android application that utilizes multiple music recognition APIs, including AudD, ACRCloud, and Shazam, to identify songs in real-time. This versatile app provides users with a comprehensive music discovery experience, leveraging the strengths of each integrated API. By harnessing the power of these services, MusicRecognizer offers a robust and accurate music recognition solution.
