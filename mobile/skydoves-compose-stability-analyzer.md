## 📝 Summary

The skydoves/compose-stability-analyzer is an open-source project that provides real-time analysis of Jetpack Compose composable functions' stability within popular Android development environments such as Android Studio and IntelliJ. This tool helps developers identify and debug potential issues in their Compose UI components, enhancing the overall stability and performance of their applications. Its integration directly into the development workflow streamlines the debugging process.
