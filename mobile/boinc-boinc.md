## 📝 Summary

The BOINC (Berkeley Open Infrastructure for Network Computing) project is an open-source platform that enables volunteer computing and grid computing, allowing individuals to contribute their computer processing power to various scientific research projects. This volunteer-based initiative empowers researchers to accelerate their studies and discoveries by harnessing the collective power of global computing resources.
