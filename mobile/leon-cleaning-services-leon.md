## 📝 Summary

The leon app is an open-source Android solution designed to remove tracking parameters from shared URLs, providing users with more control over their online privacy. This mobile app aims to simplify the process of protecting personal data by automatically scrubbing unwanted tracking identifiers from URLs. By utilizing leon, users can enhance their online security and maintain anonymity when sharing links.
