## 📝 Summary

a1ive/nwinfo is an open‑source utility that gathers detailed hardware information on Windows systems, presenting data such as CPU, memory, storage, and device specifications in an easy‑to‑read format. Designed for mobile‑oriented workflows, it helps developers and power users quickly assess a machine’s capabilities without needing third‑party tools.
