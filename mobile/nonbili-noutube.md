## 📝 Summary

NouTube is an open-source mobile application that combines the features of YouTube and YouTube Music into a single, ad-free platform. This innovative app allows users to access their favorite videos and music without interruptions, and even enables background playback for a seamless experience. By integrating both services, NouTube provides a convenient and distraction-free way to enjoy online content on-the-go.
