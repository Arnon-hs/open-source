## 📝 Summary

LibChecker is an open-source mobile app designed to provide users with a comprehensive view of the libraries used within installed apps on their device. This tool offers insights into the dependencies and components that support the apps, making it a valuable resource for developers and security researchers. By leveraging LibChecker, users can gain a deeper understanding of their device's app ecosystem.
