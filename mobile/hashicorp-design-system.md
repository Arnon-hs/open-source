## 📝 Summary

The Helios Design System is an open-source project that aims to provide a unified design language for building intuitive and user-friendly applications. Developed by HashiCorp, this design system offers a comprehensive set of guidelines, components, and tools to help designers and developers create consistent and accessible interfaces. By leveraging the Helios Design System, teams can streamline their design and development processes, resulting in more efficient and effective product creation.
