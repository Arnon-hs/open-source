## 📝 Summary

Filament is an open-source, physically based rendering engine that enables real-time 3D graphics on various platforms, including Android, iOS, Windows, Linux, macOS, and WebGL2. This powerful engine provides a high level of realism and efficiency, making it suitable for a wide range of applications such as games, simulations, and visualizations. By leveraging Filament, developers can create stunning, high-performance 3D experiences across multiple devices.
