## 📝 Summary

MpvNova is an open‑source Android TV application that integrates the libmpp media engine to provide a lightweight, hardware‑accelerated video player optimized for large‑screen, remote‑controlled playback. It offers a clean, TV‑friendly UI, support for a wide range of codecs, subtitle handling, and customizable playback controls, making it a versatile alternative to the default Android TV media player.
